## What it is
The Quickened boundary is marked by the **Thal Stones** on [[greymere-lake|Greymere lake]]. It delineates the territory of the [[the-quickened|Quickened]] / the [[the-prior|Prior]]. To cross the boundary is treated as a **natural death** — a one-way passing into the spirits' domain, not a journey one returns from.

## What's known
- The boundary frames the Greymere crisis: fishermen who strayed too far were killed by the hostile Yarrows, and townsfolk stayed off the lake for safety while the party negotiated passage beyond it (Reflections, Dakir entry 13).
- [[dakir|Dakir]] considered sailing to the **Thalstones** to investigate but feared "causing more upset" and consequences (Reflections, Dakir entry 13).
- The boundary is bound up with the legend of [[the-stormblind|the Stormblind]] (lost beyond the lake) and with [[serris-dane|Serris]]'s plan to sail past it to be "swept up" by his god.

## Open questions
- What lies beyond the boundary, and what exactly happens to those who cross — the literal death the custom implies, or passage into the Quickened's realm?
- How does the boundary relate to the broader balance the Prior maintain?
