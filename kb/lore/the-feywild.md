## What it is
The Feywild is a separate plane, home to the fey — satyrs, unicorns, dryads, leprechauns, and the powerful Archfey who rule its sanctuaries. The elves were once believed to dwell here and only visit the Material Plane (see [[the-disappearance-of-dragons]]). It is a place of beauty, glamour, and danger, where the rules of the Material Plane bend.

## What's known
- The party crossed into a Feywild sanctuary beyond the [[the-crystal-cave|Crystal Cave]] in S1.04 — the [[the-feywild-garden|Feywild garden]] built by the mage Perfora and her elven love Cerwin. Those who drink from its fountain stay forever and can never return once they leave.
- It is ruled in places by Archfey such as [[the-gardener|the Gardener]], a blue-skinned figure robed in greenery who guards the sanctuary.
- Both the Feywild and the underdark are described as **hyper-fertile** places where the [[the-uthrel|uthrel]] grows easily ([[shalfay|Shalfay]]'s prophecy, S1.04; confirmed by [[hesk|Hesk]], S2.21).
- The uthrel entered the Feywild ~900 years ago and is the reason the elves fled it (S2.21).
- Crossings between the planes are thinning and unstable — see [[the-feywild-barrier]]. As of S2.21, travel to the Feywild is **no longer possible**.

## Open questions
- What is the present state of the Feywild now that the uthrel has spread there?
- The [[the-bright-gate|Bright Gate]] (a Feywild crossing) was sealed when the elves fled; how many other such gates remain, and where do they lead? (S2.22)
