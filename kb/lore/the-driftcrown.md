## What it is
The driftcrown is a rare **bloom-Quickened** — a flowering form of the [[the-quickened|Quickened]] — treated as an **omen of fortune**. It is a sign of good favour from the spirits of the water and land.

## What's known
- A driftcrown caught [[trevick|Trevick]]'s interest at [[greymere|Greymere]]; he planned to ask [[nessa|Nessa]] to guide him to rushes so he could **weave a little flower-and-rush token** and send it back to the Yarrows (the lake Quickened) "with the same good intentions the drift crown was sent towards them" (Reflections, Trevick entry 12).
- It is bound up with the broader question of communicating with and placating the Quickened during the Greymere crisis.

## Open questions
- Is a driftcrown's appearance a genuine omen, or simply read as one by those who watch the Quickened?
- Did Trevick's gesture of goodwill reach or affect the Yarrows?
