## What it is
The Valenwyr are the peoples who settled Valenya (the Material Plane, "the Lands of the Valenwyr"): **humans, halflings, dwarves, and gnomes**. They are not a homogenous people but largely live harmoniously, often in mixed towns, though some places favour one people and some prefer to live among their own kind.

## What's known
- They are **not the first civilisation** to settle these lands — ruins and remnants of older sites are common, and the [[the-horizon-doors|Horizon Doors]] are relics of an earlier people. How the Valenwyr came to settle here is "lost to tales and legends," most of which involve a journey by sea.
- For about a thousand years Valenya has been a patchwork of small autonomous duchies and kingdoms, with [[erythar|Erythar]] the principal centre of power, mediated by a long-standing **Council of State**.
- More uncommon sentient peoples — firbolgs, goliaths, centaurs — are **not** counted among the Valenwyr; they are the indigenous [[the-prior|Prior]] (see [[the-eldryn]]), thought native to the land and concentrated in the wilder regions.
- [[mags|Mags]], a gnome of the underdark, embodies a Valenwyr subculture: deep-cave gnomes living under strict "Ordinances" against the dark (Reflections, Mags entry 20).

## Open questions

- What older civilisation left the ruins and the Horizon Doors?
