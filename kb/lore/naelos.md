## Overview
Naelos is the god of **community, oaths, bonds, gratitude, and transparent dealing**. Worship is organised through the [[the-covenant-of-naelos|Covenant of Naelos]], commonly "the Covenant," whose houses are called **Hearths** or **Threadhouses** (Hearth-Halls, Quiet-Hearths, and Hearthsteps by size). The imagery of weaving threads and binding bonds runs through the faith.

## What's known
- **Clerical hierarchy:** Witness (lay helper who observes and records vows and sacred acts); Steward / Threadkeeper (local village priest); Hearthwarden (oversees a full Hearthhall, maintains community bonds); Oathkeeper (senior regional figure) (Churches of Kaelindor).
- The faith's great festival is [[nameweave|Nameweave]], a day "sacred to honest and transparent dealing, to upholding agreements and reinforcing communal bonds, in virtue and in trust" (Reflections, Mags entry 17).
- [[mags|Mags]] (Smagslys Mørktbarn), a centuries-old gnome cleric, is the party's foremost Naelos devotee and diplomatic anchor; she tends to think "Naelos helps those who help themselves" rather than waiting for signs. The goliath paladin [[orin-njord|Orin]] is also devout.
- Mags consults the universe via methods beyond prayer — bone-casting and tea-leaf reading — to find "the eye Naelos expects her to thread" (Reflections, Mags entry 13).
