## What it is
The Irdane is a **hereditary role**: the person through whom the [[the-eldryn|Eldryn]] send their messages and warnings to the people of [[greymere|Greymere]]. It is the channel of communication between the town and the [[the-prior|Prior]] of the forest — a duty meant to be passed down a single bloodline.

## What's known
- The role **lapsed and diverged**: over the short generations of the Valenwyr the succession split, so that **both [[mathis-irdane|Mathis Irdane]] and [[serris-dane|Serris Dane]]** carry pieces of the bloodline and the role (S2.21).
- Because the line diverged, the Eldryn's warnings reached the wrong heir: **[[serris-dane|Serris]]'s prophetic dreams of storms and oblivion were the Eldryn's warnings**, arriving in a Dane who did not know he was meant to receive them (S2.14, S2.21).
- The party reconciled the two: explaining the divergent succession, they persuaded Mathis that he and Serris **together** are the Irdane and must work as a team to restore communication with the Eldryn (S2.24).
- Trevick suspects the Eldryn's long memory preserved a duty that Greymere itself forgot (Reflections, Trevick entry 17).
