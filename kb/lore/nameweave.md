## What it is
Nameweave (also written Namesweave/Namesweave) is one of the holiest festivals of [[naelos|Naelos]], god of community and oaths. It is a day "sacred to honest and transparent dealing, to upholding agreements and reinforcing communal bonds — in virtue, and in trust" (Reflections, Mags entry 17). At its core it is about people **coming together** to share food, drink, stories, and gifts.

## What's known
- Customs vary by place — [[mags|Mags]] has seen it "celebrated in a dozen different ways" — but the core is always the same (Reflections, Mags entry 17). It is widely celebrated in the cities with balls and build-up; Caspian recalls it as a village gathering where "there's a magic in it without the divine" (Reflections, Caspian entry 18).
- Traditions include the **head of household preparing breakfast**, gifts shared under the family tapestry, and an evening gathering of food, drink, and stories (Reflections, Fabian entries 18-19).
- The party celebrated Nameweave while detained in the [[the-eldryn-camp|Eldryn camp]] (S2.20). Mags hosted the gathering (secretly casting Zone of Truth over it); Fabian cooked; the party exchanged plush gifts and personal histories. The **Eldryn themselves do not observe Nameweave** (S2.20).
- For the [[the-tir-van|Tir'Van]], who already share thought directly, the festival is "almost redundant" but valued as a day when prejudice eases (Reflections, Rell entry 18). Rell's Nameweave gift to Fabian was a wordless mind-communion.
- [[nameweave-crackers]] are an associated party item.
