## What it is
Meenlocks are fey creatures that frighten their victims and convert them into more of their own kind, spreading through fear. The party encountered a meenlock nest beneath [[nerrin]] in the early arc.

## What's known
- The meenlock nest in the [[nerrin-mine|Nerrin mine]] grew alongside the [[the-uthrel|uthrel]] ("the moss"); the moss-tunnels were tied to the nest.
- When the party killed the meenlocks, **the moss-tunnels died with them** and Nerrin was cleansed (S1.04) — the mine was then reopened.
- Their fey nature links them to the [[the-feywild|Feywild]], the same source as the uthrel.

## Open questions
- Were the meenlocks drawn by the uthrel, or did they arrive together? The relationship between meenlock nests and uthrel growth is unconfirmed.
