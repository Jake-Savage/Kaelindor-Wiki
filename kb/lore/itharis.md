## Overview
Itharis is the deity of **transitions, death, change, and thresholds** — endings, transformations, and the crossing of boundaries. Worship is organised through the [[the-shadowed-path|Shadowed Path]], commonly "the Path," whose houses are **Shadowhouses** (the Hall of Silver Light).

## What's known
- **Clerical hierarchy:** Seekers (novices who tend shadowhouses and offer counsel); Walkers-in-Darkness (attend the dying, conduct death rites); Walkers-in-Shadow (lead rituals of transformation, symbolic death and rebirth); the **Stranger** (feared, revered figures who officiate the greatest transitions — coronations, exiles, the founding or dissolution of households; some say their touch can sever the bonds of a former life); and the **Passing Shadow**, appointed only when diviners indicate a great world-altering change is possible — the last served 300 years ago, during the founding of the Elenfain (Churches of Kaelindor).
- Itharis's clergy **guard the two wilderness [[the-horizon-doors|Horizon Doors]]**, which are major holy sites of hers (setting summary).
- **Overt rank is the lowest rank.** Openly-declared Itharian priests are the least of the orders; senior priests do not announce themselves. Many people belong to the Itharian orders in youth and later leave (S2.28).
- Chancellor [[oscar-wicklow|Oscar Wicklow]] is rumoured to be a **secret priest of Itharis** — as a youth he was part of the Itharian orders; he denies the rumours vehemently (S2.28 confirms the youthful tie and his denials).
- [[hethan-kroll|Hethan Kroll]]'s ritual murder bore the marks of an **Itharian crossing-ritual** — the surroundings untouched (S2.24).
- **The [[the-mirrowen|Mirrowen]] (the Tangles) is considered sacred to the Itharians** — a place built of thresholds, gateways, crossings, and ambiguity. They acknowledge its blighted nature but find it fascinating and reverent, and followers come there to "quicken" their magic (S2.28).
- Itharians regard the [[rell-aetris|Horizonborn]] with reverence and empathy for their threshold-origins, more so than they meet elsewhere; meeting one on a festival eve is taken as a good omen (S2.28).
- **Shadows Meet (the Vigil)** — an Itharian festival on the 1st of Ithar's Veil, beginning at dusk and running through the night, at which masks are worn. It is one of the few Itharian rites that is not secret; non-Itharians do not normally participate. It draws heavy pilgrimage to [[greywatch]] (S2.28).

## Open questions
- Why was the last **Passing Shadow** appointed 300 years ago, at the founding of the Elenfain — the same era as the [[the-disappearance-of-dragons|disappearance of the dragons]]? What "world-altering change" was foreseen?
- Is Wicklow truly an Itharian priest, and did the Path carry out Kroll's death-ritual? (S2.24, S2.26.)
