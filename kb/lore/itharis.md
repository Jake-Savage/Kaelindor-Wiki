## Overview
Itharis is the deity of **transitions, death, change, and thresholds** — endings, transformations, and the crossing of boundaries. Worship is organised through the [[the-shadowed-path|Shadowed Path]], commonly "the Path," whose houses are **Shadowhouses** (the Hall of Silver Light).

## What's known
- **Clerical hierarchy:** Seekers (novices who tend shadowhouses and offer counsel); Walkers-in-Darkness (attend the dying, conduct death rites); Walkers-in-Shadow (lead rituals of transformation, symbolic death and rebirth); the **Stranger** (feared, revered figures who officiate the greatest transitions — coronations, exiles, the founding or dissolution of households; some say their touch can sever the bonds of a former life); and the **Passing Shadow**, appointed only when diviners indicate a great world-altering change is possible — the last served 300 years ago, during the founding of the Elenfain (Churches of Kaelindor).
- Itharis's clergy **guard the two wilderness [[the-horizon-doors|Horizon Doors]]**, which are major holy sites of hers (setting summary).
- Chancellor [[oscar-wicklow|Oscar Wicklow]] is rumoured to be a **secret priest of Itharis** (Greymere arc).
- [[hethan-kroll|Hethan Kroll]]'s ritual murder bore the marks of an **Itharian crossing-ritual** — the surroundings untouched (S2.24).

## Open questions

- Is Wicklow truly an Itharian priest, and did the Path carry out Kroll's death-ritual? (S2.24, S2.26.)
