## What it is
The Horizon Doors are artefacts left by an older civilisation that predates the [[the-valenwyr|Valenwyr]]. Through them, areas and worlds **beyond Valenya** can be reached — including, through the hidden [[the-bright-gate|Bright Gate]], the [[the-feywild|Feywild]]. They are how it is known that other worlds exist at all.

## What's known
- There are **three publicly-known Horizon Doors**: one in [[crownspire|Crownspire]] (capital of Erythar), and two out in the wilds (setting summary).
- Beyond those three, others exist **hidden**: the [[the-eldryn|Eldryn]] keep one in their forest — the [[the-bright-gate|Bright Gate]] (Thalasyr Eren) — through which the [[the-uthrel|uthrel]] crossed (S2.22).
- The two wilderness doors are **major holy sites of [[itharis|Itharis]]**, protected by her "formidable clergy" (the [[the-shadowed-path|Shadowed Path]]) — fitting, as Itharis governs transitions and thresholds.
- They are **central to [[rell-aetris|Rell]]'s people, the [[the-tir-van|Tir'Van]]** ("Horizonborn"), who came to Valenya through such a door from a homeland they speak of only as "a battle for survival" (Reflections, Rell entry 20).
- Rell learned from the [[the-bright-gate|Bright Gate]] that planar gates may be **near-symbolic** — "markers" or "metaphysical anchors" for seals to attach to, rather than yawning physical portals — and wonders if the Crownspire door is likewise just an anchor (Reflections, Rell entry 21).

## Open questions
- **Where do the Horizon Doors lead, and can they be redirected?** Rell wonders whether a gate, "with the right knowledge and will, [could] be pointed elsewhere" (Reflections, Rell entry 21).
- **Have the Tir'Van been sealed away?** Rell fears the people of Crownspire may have **sealed the Tir'Van homeland shut** using the Crownspire door, leaving those left behind to suffer (Reflections, Rell entry 21). He believes the elder "Aerek" may have borne the knowledge to open and unseal these gates.
- **The [[the-uthrel|uthrel]] crossed through a Horizon Door** — the hidden [[the-bright-gate|Bright Gate]] — confirming Rell's instinct that it might be "corruption from the homelands" and the sign of another door (Reflections, Rell entry 20). Still open: are other hidden doors breached, and where do they lead?
- Who built them, and what older civilisation left these relics behind?
