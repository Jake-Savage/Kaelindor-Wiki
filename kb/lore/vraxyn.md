## Overview
Vraxyn is the deity (the **Ash Queen**) of **fire, destruction, and unmaking**. Her faith, the **Dance of the Burning Blade**, is known among devotees as **The Unmaking** and among ordinary folk — more fearfully — as [[the-ruin-of-vraxyn|The Ruin]]. Its houses are **Chantrys** (the Queen's Chantry), less flatteringly called **Burnhouses**.

## What's known
- **Clerical hierarchy:** Cinderborn (novices dedicated through offerings of fire or blood); Ashdancers (initiates who practice ecstatic dances of blade and flame); Flamebearers (priests who tend Burnhouses and lead rites of destruction); Fireblades (high clergy presiding over the Queen's Chantries, feared for "unleashing Vraxyn's joy in unmaking"); and **The Ash Lord**, the supreme figure — the Ash Queen's consort, whether a single person or a shifting mantle is uncertain (Churches of Kaelindor).
- It is feared as a destructive cult rather than a respectable public faith.
- Its imagery — fire, ash, blood-offerings, unmaking — strongly echoes the [[korrathian|Korrathian]] [[the-furnace-brood|Furnace Brood]], whose Bridgemarch shrine was a "blood-and-ash" altar (S2.26). Any direct link is unconfirmed.
