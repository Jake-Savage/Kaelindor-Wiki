## What it is
The Stormblind were a group of **22 followers of [[rhynharran|Rhynharran]]** lost to a supernatural storm on the lake beyond [[greymere|Greymere]]. They are remembered at [[the-stormblind-memorial|the Stormblind Memorial]]. The episode passed into local legend and devotion.

## What's known
- [[serris-dane|Serris Dane]], a fervent young Rhynharran devotee, believes that if he **recreates the last great storm** — sailing out past the [[the-quickened-boundary|boundary]] as the Stormblind did — he will be "swept up by his god" and ascend (Reflections, Caspian entry 12). He has been secretly building a **storm-boat** with the boatwright [[jorren]] to do so.
- The party (and Caspian especially) judged Serris "a result, not a cause" — a fanatic and a likely scapegoat, not the source of the crisis (Reflections, Caspian/Rell entries 12-13).
- Serris's drive turned out to be entangled with the [[the-irdane|Irdane]] role: his prophetic dreams of "tempests and oblivion" were the Eldryn's warnings reaching the wrong heir (S2.21, S2.24).
