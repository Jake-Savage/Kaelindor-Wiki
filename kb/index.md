# The Kaelindor Codex

Welcome to the campaign codex for our game set in **[[kaelindor]]**, a small alpine kingdom in
**[[valenya]]**, the Lands of the Valenwyr. This is the players' and DM's shared reference for
who we've met, where we've been, and what we're chasing.

Our party — the scholar [[caspian-talon]], the construct-sorcerer [[dakir]], the ranger
[[trevick]], the bard [[fabian-perennius]], the cleric [[mags]], the masked [[rell-aetris]],
and others — began with small heroics around Stonehaven and Bridgemarch. They have since been
drawn into something far larger: the spread of **[[the-uthrel]]**, a magic-devouring blight out
of [[the-feywild]] that destroyed Trevick's home and now threatens [[greymere]] and beyond.
