## Description
A sunblade — a sword whose blade sheds sunlight, an effective weapon against undead and especially vampires. It is a heirloom of the Torston family, having belonged to [[lord-torston|Lord Magnus Torston]]'s grandfather.

## History
[[lord-torston|Lord Torston]] gifted the blade to [[fabian-perennius|Fabian]] in S2.01 when he hired the party to hunt the supposed vampire behind [[edward-harrington|Lord Harrington]]'s bathhouse murder, since Torston could not offer a direct cash reward. Fabian wielded it through the investigation. When the hunt collapsed without a slain vampire — [[lucien]] having fled the city — Fabian had to reluctantly return the sunblade to Torston (S2.09). He and [[caspian-talon|Caspian]] immediately began scheming to reclaim it one day, leaving its status as `sought`.
