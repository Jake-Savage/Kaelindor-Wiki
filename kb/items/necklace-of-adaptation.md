## Description
A Necklace of Adaptation, which lets the wearer breathe normally and adapt to hostile environments (gas, underwater, and the like).

## History
Granted to [[trevick|Trevick]] by [[shalfay|High Mage Shalfay]] among the rewards after the Tower affair (S1.04). It passed to Trevick's owlbear companion [[muriel|Muriel]], the party's tank, who now wears it.
