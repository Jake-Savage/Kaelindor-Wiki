## Description
The magical golden lute once belonging to [[frederick-dartwell|Frederick Dartwell]], a beloved and famous bard of [[toadhop|Toadhop]]. Regarded as valuable and dangerous enough that the party deemed it too perilous to leave with ordinary villagers.

## History
Dartwell's grave was robbed of the lute (along with minor bones) by quasits serving the devil [[joster-sirit|Joster Sirit]], who collected magical artifacts (S1.05). The party, having taken the recovery job at [[queen-rowenna|Queen Rowenna]]'s Hearing Day, traced the theft to [[paliset-hall|Paliset Hall]], banished Joster back to the hells, and found the lute in his extradimensional hoard. Rather than return the dangerous instrument to [[toadhop|Toadhop]], they gave the villagers 500 gold and let [[fabian-perennius|Fabian]] claim the lute for his bardic college. It has remained his signature instrument since.
