## Description
A magical crossbow, a token of gratitude from a grieving widower.

## History
[[lucas-grovener|Lucas Grovener]], innkeeper of the Bored Weasel and a survivor of [[nerrin|Nerrin]], gave [[trevick|Trevick]] the crossbow in S1.02 after the party laid flowers on his late wife Lorna's grave and retrieved her hidden locket during the Nerrin investigation.
