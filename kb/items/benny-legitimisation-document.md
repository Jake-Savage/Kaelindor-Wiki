## Description
A signed and sealed deed in which [[edward-harrington|Lord Harrington]] names [[benny-carter|Benedict "Benny" Carter]] his natural son and heir. The document is genuine, though Benny is not in fact Harrington's biological son — Harrington truly intended him to inherit regardless.

## History
[[edward-harrington|Harrington]] left the sealed deed hidden in his study for Benny in case anything happened to him; the party recovered it during the break-in (S2.02) and deciphered its meaning (S2.03). Benny already knew of it. The party took him to the College of Arms, where the deed founded a formal petition to [[queen-rowenna|the Queen]] (S2.04); the claim was ultimately accepted and Benny legitimised as heir (S2.10).
