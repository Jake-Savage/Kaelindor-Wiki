## Description
A stuffed owlbear plushie named "Beakers." Beyond sentiment, it proved unexpectedly useful: the party used it to confuse a giant owlbear into treating Beakers as its cub, helping to lull and drive the beast off (S2.15).

## History
[[fabian-perennius|Fabian]] received the owlbear plushie as a [[nameweave|Nameweave]] gift (S2.10). During the deep-forest trek it was used to placate the attacking giant owlbear (S2.15). It ultimately passed to [[trevick|Trevick]], keeper of the owlbear [[muriel|Muriel]].
