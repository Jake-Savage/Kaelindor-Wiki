## Description
A collection of ten to twelve "books of prophecy" whose divinatory scope exceeds [[shalfay|Shalfay]]'s own. The [[deep-gnome-forge|deep gnomes]] who held them considered them worthless. Shalfay later used them to crudely answer [[caspian-talon|Caspian]]'s question about the black moss — returning a clear warning against contamination (S1.04).

## History
The deep gnomes had collected the books over time but valued them far less than the [[the-fallen-star|fallen star]]. [[shalfay|Shalfay]] sent the party to trade the star for the books (S1.03); the gnomes made the trade gladly. The party delivered the books to Shalfay, who kept them at the [[tower-of-heaven|Tower of Heaven]]. Note: in S2.23 the Tower reported the books the party donated were somehow "wrong" or "off" — a lingering, unexplained puzzle.
