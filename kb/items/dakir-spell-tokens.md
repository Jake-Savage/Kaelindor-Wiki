## Description
A set of enchanted tokens — effectively sending-stones — that allow party members to relay short messages to one another at a distance.

## History
The tokens are in regular party use; for example, [[dakir|Dakir]] and [[trevick|Trevick]] received word through them that [[caspian-talon|Caspian]] had left the clocktower and was returning home (S2.05). Shared among the party.
