## Description
A small carved wooden wren — a symbol of welcome and favour sacred to firbolgs. It signified that someone deep in the [[the-eldryn-forest|forest]] had deliberately reached out to [[trevick|Trevick]] and offered friendship. It functions as a token of standing that the [[the-eldryn|Eldryn]] recognise.

## History
The wren arrived in a soft-leather packet as the forest's response to the respectful written offering Trevick left on the trail (S2.13). The packet, delivered to him at [[nessas-cottage|Nessa's cottage]], also held [[reinas-ring|a silver-and-pale-wood ring]] (meant for another) and [[the-owl-feather|an owl feather]] (S2.14). [[nessa]] explained someone in the forest had singled Trevick out. The wren-token persuaded the Greenheart [[thalos-briarwood|Thalos Briarwood]] to commission Trevick's secret forest mission (S2.15), and when Trevick showed it to the firbolgs at the [[the-eldryn-camp|Eldryn camp]] he was welcomed warmly (S2.19), confirming it as a mark of being chosen.
