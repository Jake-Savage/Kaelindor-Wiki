## Description
An artificer device built into [[dakir|Dakir]] himself — fitting, as Dakir is a uniquely advanced [[constructs|construct]] of the [[church-of-calvyr|Church of Calvyr]]. Once finished, the device can hold low- to mid-level spells for later release.

## History
The conspirator gnome [[bartholomew-crane|Bartholomew Crane]], a former paladin of [[church-of-calvyr|Calvyr]], left artificer components for Dakir when he fled the city with [[lucien|Lucien]]; [[caspian-talon|Caspian]] delivered them on his return (S2.05). Dakir took the components to the artificer-priest [[talan-pryce|Talan Pryce]] at a [[church-of-calvyr|Church of Calvyr]] temple, who — in a side chapel — completed and installed the device, giving it the capacity to store low- to mid-level spells (S2.10). The device is permanently part of Dakir.
