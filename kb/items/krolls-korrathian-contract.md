## Description
A Korrathian ([[korrathian|Infernal]]) "contract" by which the magically untalented [[hethan-kroll|Hethan Kroll]] was granted Korrathian magic and paid — via a Crownspire bank — by the [[the-furnace-brood|Furnace Brood]]'s "Broodfather." It is hard proof linking Kroll to the Furnace Brood and the gate-tampering conspiracy.

## History
Found in [[hethan-kroll|Kroll]]'s locked box with [[krolls-map|the marked map]], an [[amulet-of-proof-against-detection|amulet]], charm/compulsion scrolls, and a Furnace Brood sigil (S2.24). [[rell-aetris|Rell]] recognised the [[the-furnace-brood|Furnace Brood]] as a brutal Korrathian faction. The contract is central evidence in [[the-korrathian-conspiracy|the Korrathian conspiracy]], retained by the party as they pursued Kroll's Bridgemarch contact.
