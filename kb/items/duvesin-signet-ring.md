## Description
A signet ring bearing the snowdrop crest of [[mayor-duvesin|Mayor Duvesin]]'s family, the last mayoral line of [[nerrin|Nerrin]].

## History
The ring was found on a meenlock corpse in the [[nerrin-mine|Nerrin mine]] (S1.01). [[lady-aldara|Lady Aldara]] asked the party to return it to Duvesin's descendants in [[bridgemarch|Bridgemarch]] (S1.02); the errand was carried out as a loose end of the early arc.
