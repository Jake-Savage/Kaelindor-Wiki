## Description
A fallen star — a celestial object that crashed to earth, described in [[shalfay|Shalfay]]'s harvested memory as "scarred by the heavens." [[shalfay|High Mage Shalfay]] believed it "the key to the tower's future" and a defence against "evil seizing the power of prophecy." To the [[deep-gnome-forge|deep gnomes]], however, it was simply the last and rarest rock needed to complete a sixty-year coming-of-age rite — one stone of every kind.

## History
The star's fall was seen in a vision harvested from [[shalfay|Shalfay]] by a memory web; both Shalfay and a [[the-shadow-blade|shadow-blade mercenary]] (working for the usurper [[pyarz|Pyarz]]) sought it (S1.02). The party recovered it from a derro necromancer camp at the crash site near [[lake-therro|Lake Therro]]. After exposing Pyarz and restoring Shalfay (S1.03), they learned the deep gnomes coveted it; Shalfay sent the party to trade the star to the gnomes in exchange for their [[books-of-prophecy|books of prophecy]]. The gnomes, who considered the prophetic books worthless, gladly made the trade to complete their rite of adulthood. The star now belongs to the deep gnomes — lost to the party.
