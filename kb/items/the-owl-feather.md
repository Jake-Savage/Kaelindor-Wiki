## Description
An owl feather, a firbolg token included with the forest's gift to [[trevick|Trevick]] — a seal accompanying the offer of welcome and favour.

## History
The feather arrived in the soft-leather packet the forest sent Trevick as a response to his trail offering, alongside [[the-carved-wren|the carved wren]] and [[reinas-ring|the silver-and-pale-wood ring]] (S2.13-S2.14). [[nessa]] explained someone in the [[the-eldryn-forest|forest]] had deliberately reached out to him.
