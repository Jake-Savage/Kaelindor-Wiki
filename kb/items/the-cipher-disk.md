## Description
A cipher disk — a tool for encoding and decoding ciphered correspondence.

## History
Recovered from [[edward-harrington|Harrington]]'s study during the party's break-in, alongside ciphered letters, a sealed letter for "Benedict," and a note reading "0950" (S2.02). [[mags]] and [[trevick|Trevick]] used it to decipher Harrington's letters, exposing [[lord-torston|Torston]]'s treason and the vampire [[lucien]] (S2.03). Kept by the party as evidence.
