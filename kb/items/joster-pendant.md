## Description
A silver pendant that belonged to [[trevick|Trevick]]'s late mother. Its emotional weight and unsettling provenance make it significant: it was sent not by family but by a devil who claims to have known Trevick's father.

## History
The devil [[joster-sirit|Joster Sirit]] — whom the party fought and banished at [[paliset-hall|Paliset Hall]], and who had recognised Trevick as "a Salmeros child" (S1.05) — resurfaced by sending [[trevick|Trevick]] a note referencing his father and enclosing his late mother's silver pendant (S2.14). Trevick showed it privately to [[dakir|Dakir]] and the pair agreed to keep it quiet for the moment. The pendant anchors [[joster-and-trevicks-past|the Joster / Trevick's-past thread]] — Trevick's heritage, his lost family, and the devil's interest in him.
