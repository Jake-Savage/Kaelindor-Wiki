## Description
A crudely drawn map marking a series of sites tied to the [[the-uthrel|uthrel]] and the planar incursion: the Mirrowen (two marks), the Branweald (Caerweth), the Stonemarch (near [[trevick|Trevick]]'s lost village), eastern Marivane, and the [[the-hallow-burn|forest fire site]] beside the [[the-bright-gate|Bright Gate]]. It charts the sites the [[the-furnace-brood|Furnace Brood]] are *probing/tracing* for signs of the incursion — where their investigative activity is, or is expected to be, found. The map reflects the Brood investigating the incursion, not causing it.

## History
Recovered from [[hethan-kroll|Kroll]]'s locked box along with [[krolls-korrathian-contract|his Korrathian contract]] and [[amulet-of-proof-against-detection|a flawed amulet]] (S2.24). The party later handed the map over to Chancellor [[oscar-wicklow|Oscar Wicklow]] during their Bridgemarch audience; Wicklow took keen interest in it and kept it — notably while lying about not knowing of the uthrel (S2.26), making its custody a live suspicion.
