## Description
A silver-and-pale-wood ring, identified by [[caspian-talon|Caspian]]'s Identify as a Ring of Protection +1 carrying druidic abjuration magic. It belongs to [[reina]], the Greenheart's missing third messenger and one of the [[the-weathered|Weathered]]. It was sized for someone other than [[trevick|Trevick]] — meant to be carried, not kept by him.

## History
The ring arrived with [[the-carved-wren|the carved wren]] and [[the-owl-feather|an owl feather]] in the packet the forest sent [[trevick|Trevick]] via [[nessas-cottage|Nessa's cottage]] (S2.14). The Greenheart [[thalos-briarwood|Thalos Briarwood]] recognised it as belonging to [[reina]], his third and missing messenger, and the proof that she might still live helped persuade him to commission Trevick's secret mission into the forest (S2.15). [[reina]] was found alive in the [[the-eldryn-camp|Eldryn camp]] (S2.18), reuniting her with the ring.
