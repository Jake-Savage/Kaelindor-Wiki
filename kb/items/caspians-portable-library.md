## Description
A portable, extradimensional library — a "library-hole" — that allows [[caspian-talon|Caspian]] to store and access a substantial collection of books while travelling. Fitting for the party's obsessive scholar.

## History
Granted by [[shalfay|High Mage Shalfay]] as one of several rewards in the days after the party restored him and resolved the missing-students affair (S1.04).
