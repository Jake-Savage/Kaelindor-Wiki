## Description
A tome of necromancy concerning the reanimation of the dead. Pursuing its power destroyed [[kiona-dumois|Kiona Dumois]]: an accident involving the Codex shattered her soul and turned her into a powerful undead. The book itself is lost; what survives in the party's hands are [[kiona-dumois|Kiona]]'s dense infernal notes about it, dangerous to read but said to yield secrets over time.

## History
The Codex Mortis is the object [[kiona-dumois|Kiona Dumois]] had built her entire existence around — her [[the-shifting-library|Shifting Library]] roamed the planes stealing books in an attempt to recover the lost Codex (S2.07). The party destroyed Kiona and collapsed her library (S2.09), but did not recover the Codex itself. [[caspian-talon|Caspian]] took Kiona's infernal notes on the Codex, which reveal secrets slowly but are perilous to study. The whereabouts of the actual book remain unknown.
