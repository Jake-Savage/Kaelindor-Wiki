## Hook
Following missing library books that might tie to the vampire scare, the party is engulfed by an unnatural shadow at the [[bridgemarch-library]] and pulled into [[the-shifting-library]] — a planar subspace ruled by the undead necromancer [[kiona-dumois]].

## Progress log
- S2.07: The party fights blood-oozes and spectral figures, learning the library is [[kiona-dumois]]'s creation: it travels between planes and nests in real libraries to steal books in her search for the [[codex-mortis]], the tome that shattered her soul.
- S2.08: Climbing the library, they fight rune-bound skeletons, solve an infernal blood-riddle (Mags's undead-tinged bone shard works), find a sacrificial chamber with a reconstructable hell portal which [[caspian-talon]] sabotages, and reach Kiona's spectral apparition.
- S2.09: In a long battle [[trevick]]'s arrows finally kill Kiona; the library collapses. The party loots her study (including [[glasses-of-comprehend-languages]], a [[brooch-of-shield]], and Kiona's [[codex-mortis|Codex Mortis]] notes for Caspian) and portals back to the Material Plane. Quest resolved.
