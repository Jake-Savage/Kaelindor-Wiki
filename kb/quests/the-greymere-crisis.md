## Hook
Chancellor [[oscar-wicklow]] hires the party (official favour + 1400 gold) to stop the "water spirits" disrupting [[greymere]]'s fishing; the [[the-quickened|Quickened]] (the "Yarrows") of [[greymere-lake]] have turned aggressive and killed two fishermen.

## Progress log
- S2.10: [[oscar-wicklow]] briefs the party on "the Prior" — the [[the-quickened|Quickened]] and the [[the-eldryn|Eldryn]] — and the Thal Stone boundary. The party reaches [[greymere]], staying at [[the-reed-and-pike]].
- S2.11: Lord [[mathis-irdane]] explains the Yarrows killed Bran Hosteigg and Orren Bell, the trouble starting around a "safety" charm from the now-missing hedge witch [[illyra-fen]]; the Greenheart [[thalos-briarwood]] counsels only patience.
- S2.12: [[nessa]] reveals the forest's trail markers have been unstable for two seasons and that she smelled a distant fire to the west — the deeper cause begins pointing west. [[thalos-briarwood]]'s own messengers to the Eldryn never returned.
- S2.13: The druid [[anwen-ireth]] confirms the Quickened are "under pressure" and points west, deep beneath the lake; many forest markers are now burnt/blackened.
- S2.15: [[illyra-fen]] reveals her charms skim wild ambient magic — the same the Yarrows feed on — and that ambient magic has been drying up for months; Caspian links it to a thinning Feywild barrier.
- S2.21: The [[the-eldryn|Eldryn]] reveal the truth: they contained an [[the-uthrel|uthrel]] outbreak by burning the forest and warding off all ambient magic, which is why the forest and lake are starving and the Quickened turned hostile. [[hesk]] agrees to ally and warn Greymere.
- S2.24: The party reconciles [[mathis-irdane]] and [[serris-dane]] as joint Irdane (see [[the-serris-mystery]]).
- S2.25: [[fabian-perennius]] sends [[hethan-kroll]]'s leaderless mercenaries south to Crownspire and returns the town's extorted gold, removing the destabilising mercenary presence.

## Open threads

- The party still owes Wicklow a report; the Quickened/lake will recover only as the Eldryn relax their wards.
