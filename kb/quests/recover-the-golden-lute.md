## Hook
At [[queen-rowenna]]'s Hearing Day, [[toadhop]] villagers escorted by [[sidney-pollard]] petition for help: the grave of bard [[frederick-dartwell]] has been robbed and [[the-golden-lute]] stolen. The bard [[fabian-perennius]] volunteers.

## Progress log
- S1.05: Investigating the grave (clawed marks, small footprints), the party deduces quasits serving a master. Freed footman [[corbin-dunn]] points to [[paliset-hall]], where the tiefling [[joster-sirit]] arrived three months ago and tightened security. Infiltrating as a troupe, the party finds [[lord-adilaire]] under thrall signing away his lands. They confront [[joster-sirit]], revealed as a devil draining subjects via enchanted portraits; he names [[trevick]] as "a Salmeros child" and claims to have known his father. Dakir's wand of lightning bolts banishes Joster to the hells. The party recovers [[the-golden-lute]] from his hoard, frees Adilaire, gifts Toadhop 500 gold, and lets Fabian claim the lute for his college.
