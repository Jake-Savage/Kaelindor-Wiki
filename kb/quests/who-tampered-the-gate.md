## Hook
At the uthrel's origin in the [[the-hallow-burn]], beside the ancient, hidden [[the-horizon-doors|Horizon Door]] [[the-bright-gate]] (Thalasyr Eren), the party finds the gate's wards were deliberately altered to allow passage and then restored — an outsider let the [[the-uthrel|uthrel]] through.

## Progress log
- S2.22: On patrol in the [[the-hallow-burn]], the party reaches the uthrel's origin beside [[the-bright-gate]], sealed ~300-350 years ago when the elves fled the Feywild. [[caspian-talon]]'s notes and [[mags]] reveal a hidden second ward-layer: someone altered the wards to cross, then replaced them, relying on no one checking. [[hesk]] agrees the work is foreign to the Eldryn (a "stranger") and will convene the Council early and watch the gate. [[rell-aetris]] shows an uncharacteristic flash of excitement at the gate, noted by Mags.
- S2.24: [[hethan-kroll]]'s box surfaces the [[the-furnace-brood|Furnace Brood]] as a lead — but they are a probe, not confirmed as the tamperer. Kroll's mission was to slip into the wood and check "if the incursion left a trace"; [[mags]]'s Speak with Dead has him say the incursion's purpose is "I do not know. No one knows" — so the Brood do not know who caused the incursion (see [[the-korrathian-conspiracy]]).
- S2.26: The Bridgemarch Korrathian cell is raided but the two cultists kill themselves; the conspiracy's full purpose stays unresolved. [[oscar-wicklow]] lies about not knowing of the uthrel and keeps Kroll's marked map.
- S2.28 (catch-up narration): [[rell-aetris|Rell]] alone advances a theory that a **unicorn** tampered with the gate — the wider party does not share it, and [[mags]] openly asks why they believe it. Rell's basis is a **centaur's account**, given during the forest negotiations, that an exceedingly rare unicorn had been seen in the forest in recent memory — regarded as exceptionally unusual. Rell connects dots the others miss: unicorns are fey-native and capable of complex magic and have no business in the material forest, yet one appeared seemingly from nowhere in an area that has just seen a [[the-feywild|Feywild]] crossing. This is distinct from the two [[the-feywild-garden|Feywild Garden]] unicorns [[stargleam]] and [[silverlily]] the founding party met (S1.04).

## Open threads
- Who physically crossed and re-sealed the gate, and how did they learn Eldryn-grade warding? The tamperer is an unknown outsider (a "stranger") — even the [[the-furnace-brood|Furnace Brood]] do not know who caused the incursion.
- **[[rell-aetris|Rell]]'s unicorn theory (his alone, S2.28):** that the rare forest unicorn a centaur reported crossed the [[the-feywild|Feywild]] gate and tampered with it — reasoning from its anomalous presence (fey-native, magic-capable, not native to the forest) in an area of a fresh crossing. The rest of the party is unconvinced; unproven. Fits Rell's private interest in the [[the-horizon-doors]] and crossings.
- The Furnace Brood are a lead but explicitly not confirmed as the tamperer; they were sent to probe/trace the incursion, not established as its cause. What does the Brood/Korrath want from the incursion?
- Is [[rell-aetris]] connected? His odd excitement at the gate and his people's tie to the [[the-horizon-doors]] are unexplained.
- What does [[oscar-wicklow]] secretly know about the uthrel, and why lie?
- Are the other crossings on [[krolls-map]] also tampered with?
