## Hook
[[hethan-kroll]]'s locked box reveals a Korrathian "contract" with the [[the-furnace-brood|Furnace Brood]] and a map of marked sites — exposing a foreign conspiracy *probing and exploiting* the uthrel incursion and the Greymere crisis. The Brood are not confirmed as the gate-tamperer: per [[mags]]'s Speak with Dead on Kroll, they do not know who caused the incursion ("I do not know. No one knows").

## Progress log
- S2.24: [[rell-aetris]] opens [[hethan-kroll|Kroll]]'s box: 11 charm/compulsion scrolls, a flawed [[amulet-of-proof-against-detection]], a Furnace Brood sigil, [[krolls-map]] (marking the Mirrowen, Branweald/Caerweth, the Stonemarch near Trevick's village, eastern Marivane, and the forest-fire site), and a [[krolls-korrathian-contract|Korrathian contract]] granting the untalented Kroll magic and pay via a Crownspire bank from the Brood's "Broodfather." Speak with Dead reveals Kroll's mission was to slip into the wood and check if "the incursion left a trace"; his next orders would come from a Bridgemarch contact (address obtained). Kroll's murder bears marks of an Itharian death-ritual.
- S2.25: [[fabian-perennius]] sends the still-charmed mercenaries south; the party races back toward [[bridgemarch]] to chase the contact.
- S2.26: Mags, Rell, and Caspian stake out the contact's warded [[the-tiefling-shop|metalworking shop]]; [[captain-thoronby]]'s squad raids it, but the two resident tieflings (posing as Korrath refugees ~9 years) kill themselves with acid. Behind the fireplace is a repeatedly-used blood/ash shrine to the [[the-furnace-brood|Furnace Brood]]. Evidence goes to [[oscar-wicklow]], who lies about not knowing of the uthrel and keeps [[krolls-map]].
- S2.27: The party returns to the [[the-tiefling-shop]] and, after Caspian (newly able to Dispel Magic) and Mags open the trapped chests, Rell uses his [[amulet-of-proof-against-detection]] to open a set of protected documents. Beneath the kitchen flagstones they find a blackened, rune-carved stone — the magic-dampener and ritual site; Caspian reads its magic as communication, the renewal of oaths/pledges of allegiance, possible tribute/offerings, and suffering inflicted partly for research. Translated at [[snowdrop-house]], the three scrolls name three Brood operations: to the (dead) [[hethan-kroll]] — guard/watch the noble [[amir-merillac]] near Fairfield in [[offendale]], suspected of double-crossing the Brood; to [[tamzin-venik]] — place beacon-tokens in northern caches toward the mountains, openly; to [[soren-dragan]] — meet a contact in [[greywatch]] to pursue a rumour, secretly. The party resolves to pursue Soren Dragan to Greywatch.
- S2.28: After a five-week overland journey (north through [[trevaryn]], then down the Drake's Road), [[trevick]] rejoins and the party arrives at [[greywatch]] in [[the-mirrowen]] on 28 Dusklin, the contact's address in hand. They enter under false names and a treasure-seeker cover story, avoiding the Perennius name and any mention of hunting a Korrathian ancient because of a possible mole in [[oscar-wicklow|Wicklow]]'s court. No contact made yet; the town is crowded for the Itharian festival [[shadows-meet|Shadows Meet]].
- S2.29: The contact is identified as **[[jorrith-potter|Jorrith Potter]]**, a widowed cook who sells cheap meals from the [[the-three-copper-hatch|Three Copper Hatch]]. The party splits to investigate: [[caspian-talon]] (invisible) and [[rell-aetris|Rell]] stake out the hatch — no agent-like customer behaviour, no blunt dampening field like the Bridgemarch shop's, Detect Magic does not flare, and [[theodore]] scouts the interior (tidy, spartan, no shrine/sigils/hidden doors, a drawing of his late wife [[meera]]); meanwhile [[mags]], [[fabian-perennius|Fabian]] and [[trevick]] gather town gossip. Over a shared lunch [[trevick]] plants one of [[mags]]'s bones in Jorrith's pocket (to locate him later), and Mags reads a faint, guarded flicker when she drops the word "brood." That evening [[rell-aetris|Rell]], using **Disguise Self** (androgynous) and impersonating the agent **[[soren-dragan|Soren]]**, gives the Brood recognition sign — a Prestidigitation-flashed sigil plus the [[amulet-of-proof-against-detection]]. Jorrith (who reads **blank to Detect Thoughts**, as if mind-shielded) recognises it, panics, and takes "Soren" inside, where he delivers the Brood's orders: travel ~40 miles south to the destroyed village of **[[kethons-fold|Kethon's Fold]]**, go underground as deep as is safe, recover anything strange that "does not belong," and bring it back to report. Jorrith is terrified and reluctant; his line **"who did they take from you?"** implies the Brood coerce agents via hostages. The party chooses **not** to turn him in (to keep a live link) and resolves to reach Kethon's Fold via an **unofficial guide** to stay off the official record, skipping the festival.

## Open threads
- What is the Furnace Brood's ultimate goal in probing the uthrel incursion? (They were sent to find if it "left a trace," not established as its cause — per Speak with Dead they do not know who caused it.)
- What is at [[kethons-fold|Kethon's Fold]] — the caves, the reported singing/crying underground, and the "something that does not belong" the Brood want recovered? Unknown; not yet tied to the uthrel or any other thread.
- Is [[jorrith-potter|Jorrith]] coerced by a hostage, and if so, whom? His "who did they take from you?" establishes that the Brood take agents' loved ones, but not who (if anyone) Jorrith has at stake. Keep separate from the gossip that his wife [[meera]] died of illness.
- How does Jorrith signal his Brood handler / how does the report get "passed on"? The party left this unprobed.
- Who actually tampered with the gate to cause the incursion? Genuinely unknown — even the Brood do not know.
- Who is the "Broodfather," and why route payment through a Crownspire bank (echoing [[expose-torston|Torston's]] Crownspire trail)?
- What is the significance of every site on [[krolls-map]] — especially the Stonemarch near Trevick's destroyed village?
- Why did [[oscar-wicklow]] lie about the uthrel, and what will he do with the map?
- What rumour is [[soren-dragan]] sent to pursue in [[greywatch]], and who is the contact there?
- Is [[amir-merillac]] really double-crossing the Brood, and what work was he doing for them?
- Do [[tamzin-venik]]'s "beacon-tokens" and the northern "caches" mark or seed the incursion sites on [[krolls-map]] (e.g. the Stonemarch)? Unconfirmed.
- What was worked at the shop's ritual stone — oaths/allegiance to the Brood, tribute, and research-suffering on whom?
