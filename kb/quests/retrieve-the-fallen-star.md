## Hook
Descending from Nerrin, the party loots a memory web that killed four [[order-of-the-tower-of-heaven|Tower of Heaven]] mages, harvesting High Mage [[shalfay]]'s vision of a star "scarred by the heavens" and orders to seek it. The druid [[derwyth]] sends them to recover it from a [[the-derro-camp|derro camp]] at the crash site.

## Progress log
- S1.02: The memory web reveals the star and a Bestiary meant for the star-watcher [[derwyth]] in [[cernant-valley]]. A [[the-shadow-blade|shadow blade]] mercenary interrogates Caspian and Dakir about the memories; both lie successfully. Derwyth identifies the memories as Shalfay's, warns of plots to oust him, takes the Bestiary as payment, and directs them to the necromancer derro at the crash site near [[lake-therro]]. The party assaults the camp, recovers the star and gold-dipped antlers, frees a disguised [[the-lamia]], and reaches the [[mountain-village]] led by [[aspen]].
- S1.03: The star is carried to [[shalfay]], who reveals its true purpose and sends the party to trade it to the deep gnomes (see [[deliver-the-star-to-the-gnomes]]).
