## Hook
[[lady-aldara]], ruler of [[stonehaven]], wants to reopen the long-closed platinum mine at [[nerrin]] to avoid raising war taxes — but the town was deserted seventy years ago after a cave-in killed half its miners and survivors began vanishing two or three at a time. She pays 750 gold to assess whether the town can be worked and to clear out whatever is there.

## Progress log
- S1.01: Hired by Lady Aldara (son and heir [[edric-vayne]] present). Survivors [[lucas-grovener]] and [[vaughn]] describe a creeping terror. At Nerrin the party feel unnatural dread, find [[mayor-duvesin]]'s journals descending into madness, books on the Feywild, and a cave-in map. In the mine they find smooth tunnels, patches of black oily moss (the [[the-uthrel|uthrel]], unrecognised), and blood-stained slabs; they destroy six meenlocks that were converting victims. Caspian's reading ties the nest to a mountain tarn with a thin Feywild boundary, triggered by the disaster's emotional trauma.
- S1.04: After the [[the-feywild-garden]] detour, the party returns to Nerrin and confirms the moss-tunnels are gone — the nest died with the meenlocks. The mine is cleansed and reopened.
