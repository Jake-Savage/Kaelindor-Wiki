## Hook
A sealed deed names dock-fighter [[benny-carter]] as [[edward-harrington]]'s natural son and heir. The party petitions the College of Arms to make the claim stick.

## Progress log
- S2.04: At the College of Arms, heraldmaster [[pellan]] begins the claim and [[fabian-perennius]] vouches on his family name — but the irregular route triggers a petition to the Queen. [[mags]] gets Benny drunk and (via [[rell-aetris]]) learns Benny is not actually Harrington's blood; the deed is genuine, so Harrington truly wanted him to inherit.
- S2.10: With the vampire scare fading, Benny's claim is accepted; [[lord-torston]] publicly buries the hatchet. Quest resolved.

## Open threads
