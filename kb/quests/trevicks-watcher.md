## Hook
When [[trevick]] summons [[hawk]] among the [[the-eldryn|Eldryn]], the firbolgs react with reverence — Hawk is no ordinary bird.

## Progress log
- S2.19: The firbolgs name [[hawk]] a "[[the-watcher|Watcher]]" — a concentrated spirit [[rhynharran]] sends to watch over those who must witness or do something important. They say it has watched [[trevick]]'s family across generations, warning it is both an honour and a burden. Trevick begins to sense Hawk is more than a beast.
- S2.21: As the Eldryn ally with the party, [[hesk]] (now a confirmed Council member) weighs Trevick's role and the meaning of the Watcher in the unfolding uthrel crisis.

## Open threads

- How does being the Eldryn's chosen go-between intersect with the Watcher's charge?
