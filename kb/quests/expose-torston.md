## Hook
Harrington's deciphered letters expose [[lord-torston]] ("Magnus") as a traitor selling state secrets; [[damar]], his charmed husband, leaked the papers. What began as a vampire hunt (see [[the-bathhouse-vampire]]) becomes a treason investigation.

## Progress log
- S2.03: Letters between Harrington and Lucien expose Torston as a traitor; [[damar]] leaked papers under charm, [[elaine-blackthorne]] is his swordhand/courier. Spy [[garron-corwen]] is caught and turned into a tip-off source.
- S2.05: Caspian delivers Lucien's evidence: Torston is falsifying records of the [[knights-of-the-silent-road]] and the war-tithe of gold and magical items, sending them by airship (Bridgemarch → Mooncross → Crownspire). The packets cannot be traced; the Crownspire buyer is unconfirmed.
- S2.06: At the estate, [[mags]] briefly frees [[damar]] from Lucien's charm with Calm Emotions; Damar confesses he willingly fed Lucien Torston's papers, names [[elaine-blackthorne]] as courier, and says Torston is fixated on the Queen's circle. [[trevick]] secretly steals concealed papers from Torston's study.
- S2.09: The stolen study papers show Torston corresponding with prominent figures in Crownspire — suspicious but not conclusive. The party decides to let the matter lie and keep watch.
- S2.27: Checking in, the party asks Torston whether his reported new position is genuine and whether he can access the conspiracy evidence. He says only that the investigation should continue, and does not comment on the position or the evidence — leaving both unconfirmed.
- S2.28 (clarified in catch-up narration): The chain of Torston's downfall is confirmed. [[benny-carter|Benny]] reported the party's suspicions to the authorities after refusing Torston's overtures, which brought Torston's conspiring to official attention. Torston was **detained for a time** and his **evidence confiscated** (not withheld from the party by choice). He then shifted from detention to an "honourable appointment" in [[crownspire]] — a real reassignment, not a death ([[mags]]'s Sending confirms he is in Crownspire and alive), widely read by the party as a "get him out of town" move. He asked the party to continue the investigation but had no evidence to give, leaving them effectively at square one beyond what [[trevick]] saw in the study (S2.06). The party resolves to keep this work quiet around Benny, whose moral objection to Torston's spying might not distinguish it from their own.

## Open threads
- Who in Crownspire is buying the secrets? The buyer is presumed to be in Crownspire but remains unproven.
- Why is Torston fixated on the Queen's circle and the [[knights-of-the-silent-road]]?
- Torston is confirmed reassigned to [[crownspire]] (S2.28) — what access or exposure does the post give him there, and how did detention become an honourable appointment? The party has not dug into it.
- Possible link to the later [[the-korrathian-conspiracy|Korrathian conspiracy]], whose payments also route through a Crownspire bank.
