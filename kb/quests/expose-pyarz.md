## Hook
The ferryman [[hadley]] reveals that High Mage [[shalfay]] is presumed dead and [[pyarz]] now acts as High Mage — but the Tower's divinations have grown vague and riddling, and Pyarz hides the rank tattoos that should change automatically with office.

## Progress log
- S1.03: The party wins over [[captain-maddock]] of the [[griswald-garrison]], who smuggles them inside in mage's robes. Caspian exposes that Pyarz bears no rank tattoos — proof [[shalfay]] still lives. In battle, with loyal mages turning, Pyarz unleashes a spectator before [[trevick]] lands the killing blow; the party recovers Pyarz's sending stone. They free the haggard Shalfay in the observatory and prove his survival to the garrison, restoring him as High Mage.
