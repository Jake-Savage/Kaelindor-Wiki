## Hook
[[shalfay]] reveals the fallen star is coveted by deep gnomes near the surface who possess 10-12 "books of prophecy" beyond his own. He sends the party to trade the star for the books.

## Progress log
- S1.03: At the [[deep-gnome-forge]], an automaton leads the party to the elders. Using Comprehend Languages they learn the gnomes have spent sixty years collecting one of every rock type for a coming-of-age rite; the star is their last rock and they consider the prophetic books worthless. The trade is made gladly. The warden [[eklash]] recognises the black moss sample but treats it as taboo, revealing the gnomes have "a similar problem" in the under-dark.
