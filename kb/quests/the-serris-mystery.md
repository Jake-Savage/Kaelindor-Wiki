## Hook
[[rell-aetris]] flags an oddly excited man near the temple of Rhynharran — [[serris-dane]], who prays fervently and seems to know more about the crisis than the temple does.

## Progress log
- S2.11: The party trails [[serris-dane]] to his home shrine; his worried parents listen as he prays.
- S2.13: [[rell-aetris]] and [[dakir]] catch Serris and the boatwright [[jorren]] secretly building a storm-worthy boat at [[the-boatshed]] — warded only to survive a storm, not to keep occupants alive — to recreate the [[the-stormblind|Stormblind]] voyage.
- S2.14: Serris's parents reveal he has suffered recurring prophetic monster-dreams for ~3 months, pre-dating the crisis; his father's line foretold weather through dreams, suggesting an ancestral gift.
- S2.21: The [[the-eldryn|Eldryn]] realise Serris's dreams are their own warnings reaching the wrong "[[the-irdane|Irdane]]": the succession diverged, so both [[mathis-irdane]] and Serris (Serris Dane) carry pieces of the bloodline.
- S2.24: The party persuades [[mathis-irdane]] he and Serris are jointly the Irdane and must work as a team; Mathis resolves to summon Serris. Quest resolved.
