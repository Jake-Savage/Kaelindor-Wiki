## Hook
[[shalfay]] asks the party to find students [[isabella-falcrae]] and [[bran-oris]], who absconded into the [[the-crystal-cave]] three months ago to escape arranged duties.

## Progress log
- S1.04: The party passes through the frozen waterfall into the [[the-feywild-garden]], meeting the satyr [[zanim]], the unicorns [[stargleam]] and [[silverlily]], and the Archfey [[the-gardener]] — who is horrified by the black moss and disintegrates the sample. They solve the maze and find Isabella and Bran charmed and happy alongside reformed thieves [[argos]] and [[hamish]]. Dakir and Caspian press the couple on whether they are happy or imprisoned; Bran convinces Isabella to leave, and the party escorts them out, the waterfall sealing behind. Back at the Tower, Isabella coldly burns her farewell letter; Bran keeps his.
