## Overview
The Feywild is a plane bordering the material plane of [[valenya]], reachable through thin points in [[the-feywild-barrier]]. It is the home of the fey — archfey, satyrs, unicorns, and other denizens — and was home to the elves before they settled the material plane.

Roughly nine hundred years ago [[the-uthrel]] entered the Feywild, drove the elves out, and now returns to the material plane carried by travellers crossing between worlds. As of S2.21, travel to the Feywild is no longer possible.

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- Archfey, satyrs, and unicorns — among the fey native to the plane.
- The elves — its former inhabitants, driven out by the Uthrel.
- [[the-feywild-garden]] — the sanctuary reached through [[the-crystal-cave]].
- Crossings into the plane: the mountain tarn above [[nerrin-mine]], [[the-faerie-ring]], and the sealed [[the-bright-gate]].

## Events here
- S1.01: The party first encounters a crossing at the mountain tarn above Nerrin Mine.
- S1.04: They reach the Feywild garden sanctuary via the Crystal Cave.
- S2.16: They encounter [[the-faerie-ring]] crossing, found withered.
- S2.21: Travel to the Feywild becomes impossible.
- S2.22: The sealed [[the-bright-gate]] is identified as the tampered crossing through which the Uthrel entered.

## DM notes
The Feywild is the origin of the Uthrel and thus central to the campaign's main quest ([[source-of-the-uthrel]]). This entry is reconciled as both a location (the plane) and a related lore concept; the lore facets — how the Uthrel entered the plane, the elves' departure, and the connection to the war — sit alongside the geography here.
