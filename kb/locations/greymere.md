## Overview
Greymere is a fishing town on [[greymere-lake]], lying northwest of [[bridgemarch]] and reached along [[the-crossway]] (roughly three days' travel). Its economy depends on the lake, which is home to the Quickened the locals call the Yarrows (see [[the-quickened]]). For generations the Yarrows merely teased fisherfolk, but they turned aggressive and deadly, overturning boats and killing two fishermen — Bran Hosteigg and Orren Bell. The disruption to fishing brought the town to crisis.

The job was brokered by Chancellor [[oscar-wicklow]] of [[bridgemarch]] (see [[the-greymere-crisis]]). Greymere's new young lord is [[mathis-irdane]], in his mid-twenties, who rules from [[irdane-hall]]. The town's spiritual life centres on the Hall of Rhynharran ([[rhynharrans-hall]]), tended by the Greenheart [[thalos-briarwood]].

## DM notes
The true cause of the crisis is not the Yarrows themselves but the [[the-uthrel]] outbreak that [[the-eldryn]] are containing by burning and warding [[the-eldryn-forest]]. Those wards cut off the ambient magic the lake's Quickened feed on, leaving them starving and aggressive (revealed S2.20–S2.21).
