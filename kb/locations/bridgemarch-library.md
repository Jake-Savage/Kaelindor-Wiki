## Overview
The Bridgemarch Library is an old library in [[bridgemarch]] where books had been mysteriously vanishing. Investigating the thefts, the party discovered it was the entry point to [[the-shifting-library]] — a planar subspace nesting within it.

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- The reading floor where the party is engulfed by the patch of unnatural shadow.

## Events here
- S2.07: Investigating the missing books, the party is engulfed by a patch of unnatural shadow and pulled into [[the-shifting-library]] subspace.
- S2.09: They emerge back here as the subspace tears itself apart after the destruction of [[kiona-dumois]].
