## Overview
Offendale is a region named in the Furnace Brood instruction-scrolls recovered in S2.27. The scrolls place the town of **Fairfield** (reached south by water), near the manor of the noble [[amir-merillac]], within it, and route [[soren-dragan]] overland through **northern Offendale** to the Tangles (Meroen) and on to [[greywatch]]. The party has not yet travelled here; the geography is known only from the scrolls.

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- **Fairfield** — a town reached south by water; near [[amir-merillac]]'s manor.
- **The Tangles / Meroen** — an area in northern Offendale on the route to [[greywatch]].
