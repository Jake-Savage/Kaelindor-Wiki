## Overview
Drake Mount is the principal seat of [[trevaryn|Trevaryn]] and the seat of its council. It is the northern end of the **Drake's Road**, which runs south into [[the-mirrowen|the Mirrowen]] and on to [[greywatch|Greywatch]]. Airships arrive at Drake Mount; pilgrims bound for Greywatch's Itharian festival commonly fly in here and travel south along the road. The party does not enter Drake Mount in S2.28 but joins the Drake's Road below it.

## What is known
- In the age of dragons, Drake Mount was a place the dragons visited very often, and its geography was built around them. The same influence shows at [[greywatch|Greywatch]], whose walls were laid out to accommodate a dragon landing-ground. (DM-established, S2.28.)
- The council at Drake Mount approves who rules in the Mirrowen, reflecting Trevaryn's effective control of the Tangles.
