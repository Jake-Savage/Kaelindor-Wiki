## Overview
The Crossway is the road linking [[bridgemarch]] and [[greymere]], a journey of about three days. An older stretch of the Crossway lies buried and magically preserved, running deep into [[the-eldryn-forest]]; this ancient section predates the agreements with [[the-eldryn]].

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- The modern surface road between Bridgemarch and Greymere.
- The buried, magically preserved ancient stretch within [[the-eldryn-forest]].

## Events here
- S2.10: The party travels the Crossway to reach [[greymere]].
- S2.18: The lost party — carrying the petrified [[mags]] — stumbles onto the buried forest stretch, which leads them toward [[the-eldryn-camp]].
- S2.25: The party races back along the Crossway toward [[bridgemarch]].
