## Overview
Lake Therro is a mountain lake near [[cernant-valley]]. This is the early-arc Cernant/mountain lake — distinct from greymere-lake. Hunters camp here near the fallen-star crash site. The necromancer-derro raiding camp ([[the-derro-camp]]) is nearby, as is the [[mountain-village]] led by [[aspen]].

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- Hunters' camps near the crash site.
- [[the-derro-camp]] — the nearby necromancer derro raiding camp.
- [[mountain-village]] — the village led by [[aspen]].

## Events here
- S1.02–03: The party passes through the lake while pursuing the fallen star ([[retrieve-the-fallen-star]]).

## DM notes
Reconciliation note: Lake Therro is the early-arc mountain/Cernant lake. It is a separate location from greymere-lake (the Greymere arc lake) — do not conflate the two.
