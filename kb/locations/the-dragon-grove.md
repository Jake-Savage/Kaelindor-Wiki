## Overview
The Dragon Grove is a "place of remembrance" within [[the-eldryn-forest]], where the magic-rich bones of a young silver dragon lie partially buried among flowers that are themselves Quickened, attuned to the grove's ambient magic (see [[the-quickened]]).

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- The partially buried bones of a young silver dragon.
- The grove's Quickened flowers, which can animate into dancing flower-creatures.

## Events here
- S2.16: [[dakir]]'s draconic magic resonates with the bones; laying hands on them animates the dancing flower-creatures, which the party fights. Dakir subdues them and takes three bones — a wing, a claw, and a tooth ([[the-dragon-bones]]) — disrupting the grove's magic (see [[dakirs-draconic-heritage]]).
