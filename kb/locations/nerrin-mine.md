## Overview
The Nerrin Mine is the platinum mine below the village of [[nerrin]], and the site of the cave-in seventy years ago. The party fought and destroyed six [[meenlocks]] here (S1.01), moving through smooth tunnels, past patches of black oily moss ([[the-uthrel]]) and dark, blood-stained slabs where the meenlocks convert their victims. A mountain tarn above the mine has a thin Feywild boundary — especially at the full moon — which spawned the fey nest.

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- The smooth meenlock tunnels and the black oily moss ([[the-uthrel]]).
- The dark, blood-stained conversion slabs.
- The mountain tarn above, where the Feywild boundary is thinnest.

## Events here
- S1.01: The party fights and destroys six [[meenlocks]] in the mine.
- S1.04: The tunnels are gone and the mine is reopened (see [[reopen-the-nerrin-mine]]).

## DM notes
The thin Feywild boundary at the tarn is the mechanism behind the infestation — and an early example of the planar crossings that recur through the campaign. The moss seen here is the same scourge later named [[the-uthrel]].
