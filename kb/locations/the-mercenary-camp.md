## Overview
The Mercenary Camp lies outside [[greymere]] and houses [[hethan-kroll]]'s sellsword band (see [[krolls-mercenaries]]). The band offered to "drive back" the Quickened for coin, an offer Lord [[mathis-irdane]] refused.

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- [[hethan-kroll]] — the mercenary captain (later murdered).
- [[annet-brigg]] — succeeds Kroll in command of the still-charmed band.
- The band of [[krolls-mercenaries]].

## Events here
- S2.14: Kroll's band arrives and makes camp.
- S2.23: Kroll is found murdered in his tent, killed by a single blow bearing Itharian death-ritual marks, the tent otherwise untouched. [[dakir]] loots his records and a locked box (the [[krolls-korrathian-contract]] and [[krolls-map]]).
- S2.25: [[annet-brigg]] now leads the still-charmed band; [[fabian-perennius]] sends them south to Crownspire with a writ, returning the town's gold.

## DM notes
Kroll was a secret Furnace Brood agent (see [[the-furnace-brood]]). His band remains magically loyal to him even after his death — hence they accept Annet Brigg and follow Fabian's writ without resistance.
