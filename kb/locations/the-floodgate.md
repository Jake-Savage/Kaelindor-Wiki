## Overview
The Floodgate is a pub in [[bridgemarch]], south of the river, where [[nelly-fairchild]] works. It served as an early lead in [[the-bathhouse-vampire]] investigation, since Harrington was known to keep company with Nelly here.

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- [[nelly-fairchild]] — works here; was [[edward-harrington]]'s companion.
- Tess — Nelly's companion, present when the party gathers intel.

## Events here
- S2.01: The Floodgate is named as Nelly's workplace.
- S2.02: The party charms [[nelly-fairchild]] and her companion Tess for intel on [[edward-harrington]], learning that he patronised the fighting ring [[the-pit]] and backed the fighter [[benny-carter]], and that he meant to take damaging information to Princess [[princess-orlaith]].
