## Overview
Crownspire is the capital city of [[erythar]] and the principal centre of power in all of [[valenya]]. As the hub of the war effort, it is where the kingdoms send the best of their resources for the fight against Thal'Mirrith (see [[the-bleeding-years]]). The city is also home to one of the three [[the-horizon-doors]], relics of an older civilisation that grant passage to other worlds.

The party has never visited Crownspire, yet it looms over several plot threads. The airship route from the capital of Kaelindor runs Bridgemarch → Mooncross → Crownspire (S2.05).

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- The [[the-advardis-family]] — a Crownspire sorcerer bloodline.
- One of the three [[the-horizon-doors]] — kept here in the capital.
- Quintus Palaveus — brother of [[fabian-perennius]] (who is himself the seventh son of a Crownspire dynasty); the Furnace Brood's mercenaries are sent here to him (S2.25).

## Events here
- S2.05: The airship route out of Bridgemarch is established as running through Mooncross to Crownspire.
- S2.25: The party learns the Furnace Brood pays [[hethan-kroll|Kroll]] via a Crownspire bank and that its mercenaries are dispatched here to Fabian's brother Quintus Palaveus.

## DM notes
Crownspire is the receiving end of [[lord-torston]]'s treason: he sends falsified war-tithe and [[knights-of-the-silent-road]] records to a contact in the city ([[expose-torston]]). Combined with the Furnace Brood's Crownspire bank payments, the city is the financial and political nexus of [[the-korrathian-conspiracy]]. Fabian's family dynasty here is a potential hook into that web.
