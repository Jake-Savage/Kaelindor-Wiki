## Overview
The Feywild Garden is a beautiful sanctuary beyond the frozen waterfall of [[the-crystal-cave]], part of [[the-feywild]]; it was built by the mage Perfora and her elven love Cerwin. Those who drink from its fountain stay and can never return once they leave.

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- **[[zanim]]** — leader of the revelling satyrs.
- **[[stargleam]]** (black) and **[[silverlily]]** (white) — the garden's unicorns.
- **[[the-gardener]]** — the blue-skinned Archfey, robed in greenery, who guards the sanctuary.
- **[[argos]]** and **[[hamish]]** — reformed thieves who chose to stay.
- A maze — woven of dog-rose, mistletoe, oak, and shamrock from leprechauns, dryads, barkburrs, and fairies — leading to the palace where the students were charmed.

## Events here
- S1.04: The unicorns are horrified by the moss ([[the-uthrel]]) and [[the-gardener]] disintegrates the sample, calling it a grave danger; the party persuades the runaway students [[isabella-falcrae]] and [[bran-oris]] to leave.

## DM notes
The Archfey's reaction to the moss — disintegrating it and naming it a grave danger — is one of the strongest early signals that [[the-uthrel]] is no ordinary blight, foreshadowing its central role in Chapter 2.
