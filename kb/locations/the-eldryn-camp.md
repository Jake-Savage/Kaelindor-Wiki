## Overview
The Eldryn Camp is a temporary, hidden mobilisation camp of centaurs and firbolgs deep within [[the-eldryn-forest]], from which [[the-eldryn]] coordinate their handling of the [[the-uthrel]] crisis. It holds upwards of fifty Eldryn organised into six- to eight-person patrols with many spellcasters; supplies are thin and the whole encampment is hastily improvised. It is led by [[hesk]], with Captain [[ethis]] as his military second.

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- [[hesk]] — centaur Eldryn council member and camp leader.
- [[ethis]] — centaur captain, Hesk's military second.
- [[he-who-rests-where-the-path-narrows]] — firbolg who explains the Watcher to Trevick.
- The surviving Weathered [[reina]] and [[elset]], housed here.

## Events here
- S2.18: Centaurs cure the petrified [[mags]] and bring the party to the camp; [[hesk]] extracts an oath of non-interference. The surviving Weathered [[reina]] and [[elset]] are housed here.
- S2.19: The firbolgs (including [[he-who-rests-where-the-path-narrows]]) revere [[trevick]]'s [[hawk]] as a "Watcher" (see [[the-watcher]] and [[trevicks-watcher]]); the party covertly maps the camp.
- S2.20: At a Nameweave, [[trevick]]'s account of the "black moss" throws the camp into alarm — and they name it [[the-uthrel]].
- S2.21: [[hesk]] reveals the truth of the uthrel, teaches its destruction, and allies with the party.
- S2.22: A patrol sets out for [[the-hallow-burn]] and [[the-bright-gate]].
