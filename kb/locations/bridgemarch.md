## Overview
Bridgemarch is the wealthy, provincial capital of [[kaelindor]], built around the ancient citadel known as the Silver Keep. A river divides the city into a northern half and the rougher quarters "south of the river," and its districts include the Palace District and a busy artisan/metalworking quarter. Queen [[queen-rowenna]] rules from here and holds a monthly open Hearing Day where citizens may bring petitions directly before the throne.

The city has served as the party's hub since S1.05, when they first attended a Hearing Day. Their home, [[snowdrop-house]], is here. Bridgemarch is well connected: an airship port runs the route Bridgemarch → Mooncross → [[crownspire]] (S2.05), and roads link it to [[greymere]] and [[toadhop]].
