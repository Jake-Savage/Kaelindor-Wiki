## Overview
Kaelindor is a small mountain kingdom in the north of [[valenya]]. Its northern border is sealed by impassable peaks, and its climate is broadly alpine. The capital is [[bridgemarch]], a wealthy provincial city built around the ancient citadel known as the Silver Keep; other significant towns include [[stonehaven]], where the party's adventure began (S1.01).

Being far from the war's front lines, Kaelindor is largely peaceful — but its great wilderness produces frequent magical problems. The crown retains adventurers and mercenaries to deal with them, and local lords keep their own retainers for trouble closer to home.

Rather than send soldiers, Kaelindor tithes the war effort in gold and magical items. It is mysteriously wealthy, with rumours of untold reserves hidden in the Silver Keep.

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- Queen [[queen-rowenna]] (Rowenna Halvaran), official title Warden of the Silent Road — late middle age, wise and prudent. She holds a monthly open Hearing Day at which any subject may petition her.
- Princess [[princess-orlaith]] (Orlaith), the heir — a hothead with a formidable martial reputation, widely thought over-eager for the throne.
- The [[knights-of-the-silent-road]] — the Queen's elite.
- [[bridgemarch]] — the capital and party hub, built around the Silver Keep.
- [[stonehaven]] — the town where the campaign began.
- [[greymere]] — a lakeside settlement within the kingdom, later beset by the Uthrel.

## Events here
- The party's adventures began in Stonehaven (S1.01) and have ranged across the kingdom since.

## DM notes
Kaelindor's mysterious wealth — the rumoured reserves in the Silver Keep — is an open thread. Its war-tithe of gold and magical items is precisely what [[lord-torston]] is selling to a buyer in Crownspire ([[expose-torston]]), and that thread feeds into [[the-korrathian-conspiracy]].
