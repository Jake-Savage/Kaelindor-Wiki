## Overview
The Underdark is the vast network of lightless, dripping caverns where [[mags]] grew up among
gnomish folk — a world "always one bad harvest away from famine," safe from theft only because
it held nothing worth stealing. Survival there depends on tightly controlled population, careful
offerings to the nameless things that haunt the dark, and never disturbing the delicate balance
of the deep. Mags's people kept rigid ordinances for those who dwell beneath the earth.

## Notable inhabitants & sites
Gnomish communities; the "nameless things that made the dark somehow darker." It is also named
in the gnomish nursery rhyme and the children's warning that the [[the-uthrel]] "walks the
underways and waits for you beneath."

## DM notes
The Underdark's lore (Mags's *Ordinances for We Þat Dwell Beneþe Þe Eorþe* and the Uthrel
nursery rhyme) hints the Uthrel was known to the deep gnomes long before the party met it —
a possible thread back to [[source-of-the-uthrel]]. See [[the-feywild]] for the contrasting
"hyper-fertile" planes the moss favours.
