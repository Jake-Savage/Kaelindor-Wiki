## Overview
The Three Copper Hatch is the name everyone uses for **Potter's Pots**, the meal business
[[jorrith-potter|Jorrith Potter]] runs out of his house in [[greywatch]]. It is not a restaurant
but a hatch in the wall of a small house, from which Jorrith sells very cheap hot food (about three
coppers a bowl). It sits in one of the poorer quarters — the relatively new, higgledy-piggledy
district built over the old dragon-landing ground, where mismatched houses are crammed in and the
streets are narrow. It is the **address named in [[soren-dragan]]'s scroll**, and Jorrith is the
[[the-furnace-brood|Furnace Brood]]'s contact here (see [[the-korrathian-conspiracy]]).

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- [[jorrith-potter|Jorrith Potter]] — the cook and Brood contact; lives and works here alone.
- The house: two rooms downstairs plus a privy (a kitchen onto the hatch, and a small living
  area) and a single upstairs room (half-width, sloping roof) with a bedroom. A side door serves
  shorter customers (gnomes, halflings); a narrow alley runs down one side; a back door opens onto
  a small yard with a laundry line; windows at the back and upstairs.

## Events here
- S2.29: The party stakes out and investigates the hatch. [[caspian-talon]] (invisible) watches
  the comings and goings and finds no agent-like behaviour; [[rell-aetris|Rell]] probes for a
  concealment field and finds nothing as blunt as the Bridgemarch shop's (inconclusive whether
  anything subtler is present). [[caspian-talon|Caspian]]'s Detect Magic near the hatch does not
  flare. His familiar [[theodore]] scouts the interior: tidy, spartan, no shrine, sigils, or
  hidden doors; a framed drawing of [[meera]] by the bed. [[mags]], [[fabian-perennius|Fabian]] and
  [[trevick]] eat here (Caspian watching invisibly, Rell staying at his vantage), and Trevick plants
  one of Mags's bones in Jorrith's pocket. That evening [[rell-aetris|Rell]],
  disguised as [[soren-dragan|Soren]], gives the Brood sign and is taken inside to receive the
  [[kethons-fold|Kethon's Fold]] instructions.

## DM notes
- The property carries **no blunt dampening field** like the Bridgemarch [[the-tiefling-shop]];
  only [[jorrith-potter|Jorrith]] himself reads as mind-shielded. Whether a subtler masking exists
  is unresolved.
