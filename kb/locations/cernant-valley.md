## Overview
Cernant Valley is a mountain valley reached after Nerrin. It is home to [[derwyth]], a self-exiled elf druid and "star-watcher" who fled the elvish empire. The party came here seeking her aid in reading the memory-web they carried.

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- **Derwyth's homestead** — guarded by her common-speaking giant eagles [[avri]] and [[ivo]].
- **[[selice]]** — Derwyth's sabretooth tiger, befriended by Trevick.
- Nearby lie [[lake-therro]] and [[the-derro-camp]].

## Events here
- S1.02: [[derwyth]] identifies the memory-web's memories as those of High Mage [[shalfay]]; she accepts the Bestiary as payment to locate the fallen star, directs the party to [[the-derro-camp]], and names Rhynharran's "hand" in the region's eighteen months of unrest.

## DM notes
Derwyth's reading of the memories ties the early arc to High Mage [[shalfay]] and the [[tower-of-heaven]], and her remark about Rhynharran's "hand" in the unrest gestures toward the wider forces (the Warding, the Eldryn) that come to the fore in Chapter 2.
