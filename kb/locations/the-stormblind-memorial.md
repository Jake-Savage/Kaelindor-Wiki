## Overview
The Stormblind Memorial at [[greymere]] commemorates [[the-stormblind]]: 22 followers of Rhynharran who, centuries ago, were lost trying to calm a supernatural storm. They left behind no bodies and no wreckage.

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- The memorial itself, dedicated to the 22 lost followers of Rhynharran.

## Events here
- S2.11: The memorial and the legend of [[the-stormblind]] are introduced.

## DM notes
The Stormblind legend underlies [[serris-dane]]'s obsession with recreating the doomed voyage (see [[the-boatshed]] and [[the-serris-mystery]]).
