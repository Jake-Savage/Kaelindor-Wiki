## Overview
Harrington's Townhouse is the [[bridgemarch]] residence of the murdered Lord [[edward-harrington]]. It remains guarded after his death. Its study held the ciphered evidence that broke open both [[expose-torston]] and [[bennys-inheritance]].

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- The study, holding ciphered letters and a hidden compartment.
- Guards posted after Harrington's death.

## Events here
- S2.02: [[benny-carter]] leads [[rell-aetris]] and [[dakir]] inside. In the study they recover ciphered letters, [[the-cipher-disk]], a hidden compartment of papers (one addressed to "Benedict" — Benny's legitimisation deed, [[benny-legitimisation-document]]), and a note reading "0950".
- S2.03: The deciphered letters — exchanged between [[edward-harrington]] and [[lucien]] — expose Lord [[lord-torston]]'s treason and the conspiracy (see [[expose-torston]] and [[bennys-inheritance]]).
