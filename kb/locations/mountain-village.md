## Overview
The Mountain Village is a small village by [[lake-therro]], led by [[aspen]]. Its people had been raided by the necromancer derro of [[the-derro-camp]] before the party intervened.

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- **[[aspen]]** — the village's leader.

## Events here
