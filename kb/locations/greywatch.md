## Overview
Greywatch is named in a Furnace Brood instruction-scroll (S2.27) as the place where [[soren-dragan]] is to meet a contact and pursue a rumour, reached overland through northern [[offendale|Offendale]] and the Tangles (Meroen). After translating the scrolls, the party resolves to pursue Soren Dragan to Greywatch. It has not yet been visited; all that is known is from the scroll.
