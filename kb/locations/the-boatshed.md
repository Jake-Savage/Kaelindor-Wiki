## Overview
The Boatshed is a workshop at [[greymere]] on the shore of [[greymere-lake]] where [[serris-dane]] and the boatwright [[jorren]] secretly work to strengthen a small rowboat with "storm marks."

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- [[serris-dane]] — the prophetic dreamer driving the secret project.
- [[jorren]] — the boatwright helping warding the boat.

## Events here
- S2.13: [[rell-aetris]] and [[dakir]] spy on Serris and Jorren and discover the boat is warded only to stay afloat in storms — with no provision to keep its occupants alive. Serris means to recreate the lost [[the-stormblind]] expedition.

## DM notes
Serris's plan to sail into a supernatural storm in this boat is almost certainly fatal — the craft is built to survive the storm, not the people aboard it. This ties directly to [[the-serris-mystery]] and his prophetic dreams.
