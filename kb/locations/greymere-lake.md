## Overview
Greymere Lake is the body of water that the town of [[greymere]] depends on for its fishing trade. (This is Greymere's own lake — distinct from [[lake-therro]], the mountain lake of the early arc.) It is home to the Quickened called the Yarrows — otter-like creatures with barbed tentacles (see [[the-quickened]]) — and to the short-lived bloom-spirits known as "driftcrowns" ([[the-driftcrown]]), regarded as omens of fortune.

For generations the Yarrows only teased fisherfolk, but they turned deadly, overturning boats and killing the fishermen Bran Hosteigg and Orren Bell. Deeper beneath the lake, far to the west, larger Quickened are said to dwell.

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- The Yarrows — the otter-like, barb-tentacled Quickened of the lake (see [[the-quickened]]).
- Driftcrowns ([[the-driftcrown]]) — rare bloom-Quickened that surface here as omens.
- [[anwen-ireth]] — druid who communes with the Quickened and reads their distress.

## Events here
- S2.11: [[mags]] casts Water Walk to walk out onto the lake, greet the Quickened, and make offerings to them.
- S2.13: With the druid [[anwen-ireth]], the party observes the troubled Yarrows; she hints the cause lies far to the west, deep beneath the lake where larger Quickened dwell.

## DM notes
The lake's Quickened are starving because the wards and controlled burn that [[the-eldryn]] maintain in [[the-eldryn-forest]] cut off the ambient magic they feed on (revealed S2.21). Their aggression is a symptom of that magic-starvation, not a cause in itself.
