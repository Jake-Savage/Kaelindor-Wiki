## Overview
Snowdrop House is the party's home base in [[bridgemarch]], named the party home in S1.05 and prominent through Chapter 2. It takes its name from the snowdrop motif (compare the Duvesin snowdrop signet, [[duvesin-signet-ring]]). The party resides here between missions.

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- The party themselves, who lodge here between jobs.

## Events here
- The house recurs throughout Chapter 2:
- S2.01: Fabian receives Torston's summons.
- S2.03: The party deciphers Harrington's letters and hides [[benny-carter]] here.
- S2.04: [[caspian-talon]] slips away from here to meet Lucien.
- S2.10: Nameweave gifts are exchanged and the Greymere job is taken.

## DM notes
Snowdrop House is the recurring base of operations for Chapter 2; its name nods back to the Duvesin signet of the early arc, a small thematic thread linking the two chapters.
