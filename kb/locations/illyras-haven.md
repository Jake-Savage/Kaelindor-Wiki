## Overview
A secluded dwelling deep in [[the-eldryn-forest]] where the elf-blooded hedge-witch
[[illyra-fen]] withdrew after the death tied to one of her charms. Hard to find by design, it
sits within the shifting markers of the forest the party learned to read from [[nessa]].

## Events here
- S2.15: The party track down Illyra, recovering answers about her wild-magic charms and the
  charm linked to the Yarrow attacks.

## DM notes
Illyra's seclusion is self-imposed guilt as much as hiding; her presence in the deepwood is one
of the threads of the [[the-greymere-crisis]].
