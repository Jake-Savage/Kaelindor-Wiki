## Overview
The Faerie Ring is a circle of shrivelled mushrooms in [[the-eldryn-forest]], marking a thin point in the planar boundary (see [[the-feywild-barrier]]). An unfamiliar mould grows here — a sign that ambient magic may be being lost at this spot, foreshadowing [[the-uthrel]].

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- The withered ring of mushrooms and its strange mould.
- A mist creature bound within the ring.
- Bark-kin and "hanging fellow" predators lurking in the surrounding trees.

## Events here
- S2.16: A mist creature forms inside the ring and drains [[dakir]]'s magic; bark-kin and "hanging fellow" predators ambush from the trees.
- S2.17: In a brutal fight, bark-kin venom petrifies [[mags]] into wood; the mist creature possesses [[fabian-perennius]] until Mags's Lesser Restoration banishes it, triggering Fabian's [[fabians-radiant-magic]]. The party avoids destroying the ring, since doing so would unbind the mist creature.

## DM notes
The ring is a thin point in [[the-feywild-barrier]] and an early, unrecognised sign of [[the-uthrel]]'s magic-loss. Destroying the ring would free the mist creature, so it is left intact.
