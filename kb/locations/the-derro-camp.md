## Overview
The Derro Camp is a derro raiding camp at the fallen star's crash site near [[lake-therro]]. The derro here are necromancers. The camp held a captive who proved to be [[the-lamia]] in disguise, and a treasury containing the fallen star ([[the-fallen-star]]) and gold-dipped antlers.

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- The necromancer derro and their zombies.
- The flooded tunnels leading in.
- The treasury holding [[the-fallen-star]] and gold-dipped antlers.
- A captive who was secretly [[the-lamia]].
- A beaver dam beyond which lies the [[mountain-village]].

## Events here
- S1.02: The party assaults the camp through flooded tunnels, fighting derro and zombies, recovers [[the-fallen-star]], and frees [[the-lamia]] (who departs to feed on the dead derro). Beyond a beaver dam they find the [[mountain-village]].

## DM notes
Freeing [[the-lamia]] is a morally grey beat — she leaves to feed on the dead derro. The recovered [[the-fallen-star]] becomes the trade good that opens the deep-gnome forge ([[deliver-the-star-to-the-gnomes]]).
