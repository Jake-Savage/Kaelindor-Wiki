## Overview
Kethon's Fold (also heard "Cethan's Fold"/"Catherine's Fold") is a village roughly **40 miles
south of [[greywatch]]** in [[the-mirrowen|the Mirrowen]]. About **ten weeks ago** (around the
beginning or middle of Ashend) it was **destroyed** — swallowed by a landslip or sinkhole, the
ground simply giving way. Such collapses happen from time to time in the Mirrowen, where the land
is unstable. It is the destination the [[the-furnace-brood|Furnace Brood]] assigned to its agent
[[soren-dragan|Soren]], and therefore the party's next objective (see
[[the-korrathian-conspiracy]]).

## What is known
From the instructions [[jorrith-potter|Jorrith]] passes to the disguised [[rell-aetris|Rell]]
(S2.29):

- There are **stories of caves, passages, or holes** by which one can get underground into the
  buried village.
- There are **stories of people heard singing or crying** somewhere underneath. Whether anyone
  truly heard this is unestablished.
- The Brood want their agent to go **as deep as is safe**, find anything **strange that "does not
  belong"** to such a village (treasure, or anything anomalous), and bring it back to Jorrith to
  report — so the Brood can judge whether to send more people.
- It lies in unstable, dangerous country; a local **guide** is needed to reach it safely.

## Events here
- S2.29: Named as the target of the Brood's instructions; the party resolves to travel there
  (with an unofficial guide) and investigate. Not yet visited.

## DM notes
- **What is actually down there is unknown** — record only the reported stories (caves; singing
  or crying; "something that does not belong"). Do not assume a connection to the
  [[the-uthrel|uthrel]], the [[the-feywild|Feywild]], or any other known thread until established.
