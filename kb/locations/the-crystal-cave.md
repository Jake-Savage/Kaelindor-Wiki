## Overview
The Crystal Cave was created 250 years ago by the Tower mage Perfora and her elven love Cerwin as a refuge. It contains a deadly mud field and a frozen waterfall that grants sanctuary, leading through to [[the-feywild-garden]].

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- The deadly mud field.
- The frozen waterfall — a sanctuary gateway that seals behind those who leave.

## Events here
- S1.04: The party clears the mud field and passes through the waterfall into [[the-feywild-garden]] to find the runaway students; the waterfall seals behind those who leave.

## DM notes
The cave is the gateway into the Feywild sanctuary and another instance of a planar crossing in the early arc. The one-way sealing of the waterfall is a hazard for anyone who lingers.
