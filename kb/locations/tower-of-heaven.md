## Overview
The Tower of Heaven is the seat of the arcane and divination order (see faction [[order-of-the-tower-of-heaven]]) and a major hub of the early arc. High Mage [[shalfay]] leads it, and the gnome [[griswald-garrison]] mercenaries — under Captain [[captain-maddock]] — guard it. The Tower is reached via the ferryman [[hadley]], who requires Shalfay's passphrase. Its divinations require a great leatherbound book.

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- **[[shalfay]]** — High Mage who leads the Tower.
- **[[captain-maddock]]** and the [[griswald-garrison]] — the gnome mercenary guard.
- **[[hadley]]** — the ferryman who brings petitioners across.
- The scholars' hall, the observatory, and a lodging house for petitioners (such as [[leonard-advardis]]).

## Events here
- S1.03: The party exposes the usurper [[pyarz]] — who had faked Shalfay's death and lacked the order's rank tattoos — kills him and his spectator, and frees [[shalfay]] ([[expose-pyarz]]).
- S1.04: Shalfay sends the party to find the runaway students [[isabella-falcrae]] and [[bran-oris]] ([[find-the-missing-students]]) via [[the-crystal-cave]], and rewards them with [[caspians-portable-library]], the [[necklace-of-adaptation]], the [[wand-of-lightning-bolts]], and [[the-folding-boat]]; she uses the [[books-of-prophecy]] to warn of the moss ([[the-uthrel]]).
- S2.23: Via a Sending, the party learns that the [[books-of-prophecy]] they donated are somehow "wrong" or off — a future puzzle.

## DM notes
The donated prophecy books being "wrong" (S2.23) is an unresolved lead and a likely future hook tying the early arc's divinations back into the Chapter 2 storyline.
