## Overview
The Eldryn Forest — the Bramweald — is the ancient forest west of [[greymere]], guarded by [[the-eldryn]] (centaurs, goliaths, and firbolgs). It was once far larger than it is today. Its trail markers, which firbolg lore knows how to read, shift unstably, and many are now burnt and blackened; more and more areas have become forbidden. [[nessa]] serves as the party's guide into the woods.

The forest is the front line of the Eldryn's hidden effort to contain an outbreak of [[the-uthrel]], which they fight by burning afflicted ground and warding off all ambient magic — a desperate measure that starves both the forest and [[greymere-lake]].

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- [[the-eldryn]] — the Prior people who guard the forest.
- [[nessa]] — the firbolg-blooded guide who leads the party in.
- [[illyra-fen]] — the elf-blooded hedge-witch hiding in a haven deep within the woods.
- Sites within: [[the-dragon-grove]], [[the-faerie-ring]], [[the-eldryn-camp]], [[the-hallow-burn]] (the burnt containment zone), and [[the-bright-gate]].

## DM notes
The forest is the front line of the Eldryn's secret war against the [[the-uthrel]]. The magic-starvation that drives the Yarrows of [[greymere-lake]] aggressive is a direct consequence of the containment effort here.
