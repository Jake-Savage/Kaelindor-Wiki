## Overview
The Hallow Burn is the burnt containment zone within [[the-eldryn-forest]] — a "balled" area roughly a kilometre across, deliberately burned and ringed by anti-magic wards. It is magic-starved and scattered with animal and pig bones, evidence that this was not a purely magical fire. At its heart lies the origin of [[the-uthrel]], beside [[the-bright-gate]].

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- The anti-magic ward-ring maintained by [[the-eldryn]].
- The scattered animal and pig bones across the burn.
- The uthrel's origin point, beside [[the-bright-gate]].

## Events here
- S2.20: [[hawk]] and [[theodore]] scout the burn. The Eldryn had debated in "parliament" whether to burn it at all, some arguing the perimeter was too large and that the Quickened were being starved by it.
- S2.22: A patrol takes the party in to observe the warding; at its heart is the [[the-uthrel]]'s origin beside [[the-bright-gate]].

## DM notes
The burn and its wards are the direct reason [[greymere-lake]] and [[the-eldryn-forest]] are magic-starved — the containment that protects against the uthrel is also what is driving the Quickened to aggression.
