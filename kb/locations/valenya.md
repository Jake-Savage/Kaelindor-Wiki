## Overview
Valenya is the common name for the material plane overall — "the Lands of the Valenwyr." For roughly a thousand years it has been a patchwork of small-to-medium autonomous duchies and kingdoms, each keeping its own laws. A single larger kingdom, [[erythar]], sits on the southern coast and is recognised as the principal centre of power.

The [[the-valenwyr]] — humans, halflings, dwarves, and gnomes — are not the first civilisation to settle these lands; ancient ruins are common across the world. Legend holds the Valenwyr arrived by sea. Other worlds are reachable from Valenya only through [[the-horizon-doors]], relics of an older civilisation; three are known — one in [[crownspire]] and two in the wilds, both kept as holy sites of [[itharis]].

A Council of State, with representatives of all the kingdoms, decides matters that require cooperation — common weights and coinage among them — and mediates disputes between rulers.

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- The [[the-valenwyr]] — the settling peoples (humans, halflings, dwarves, gnomes) who give the lands their name.
- [[erythar]] — the principal power on the southern coast, leading the Erythian League in the war.
- [[kaelindor]] — a small mountain kingdom in the north, far from the front lines and home base of the party.
- The three [[the-horizon-doors]] — the only known means of reaching other worlds.

## Events here
- The whole campaign to date takes place within Valenya, from Stonehaven onward (S1.01).
- Roughly 300 years ago the dragons vanished ([[the-disappearance-of-dragons]]). Around the same era the elves settled the northeastern Wilds (the Elenfain) and founded Mirrithiel, while priests of [[calvyr]] developed airships, compasses, and constructs.
- About 120 years ago Archon Thaloriel seized Mirrithiel, renamed it Thal'Mirrith, and struck south — beginning the ongoing war, [[the-bleeding-years]], now held by the Erythian League at the Stone March.

## DM notes
The Horizon Doors and the deep history of who came before the Valenwyr are load-bearing for [[rell-aetris]]'s people, the Tir'Van/Horizonborn, and connect to the wider cosmology the party has only begun to glimpse.
