## Overview
Toadhop is a village whose people — escorted by [[sidney-pollard]] — petitioned Queen [[queen-rowenna]] at Hearing Day: the grave of the beloved bard [[frederick-dartwell]] had been robbed and his lute ([[the-golden-lute]]) stolen.

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- **Dartwell's robbed grave** and the village inn.
- **[[todd-macby]]** and **[[jory-pike]]** — scavengers; Jory witnessed the quasits behind the theft.
- **[[corbin-dunn]]** — a freed footman and informant.

## Events here
- S1.05: The party investigates the grave (a quasit theft), traces it to [[paliset-hall]] and the devil [[joster-sirit]] ([[recover-the-golden-lute]]); afterward the party gifts the villagers 500 gold.

## DM notes
The grave-robbery quest opens onto the Paliset Hall confrontation and, through [[joster-sirit]], the long thread of Trevick's father ([[joster-and-trevicks-past]]).
