## Overview
Nessa's Cottage sits on the western edge of [[greymere]], where the town gives way to the wilds. It is the home of [[nessa]], the firbolg-blooded hedge-witch, potion-maker, and the party's guide into [[the-eldryn-forest]]. The cottage serves as the threshold between the town and the deep woods.

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- [[nessa]] — firbolg-blooded hedge-witch and potion-maker; reads the forest's trail markers and guides the party.

## Events here
- S2.12: [[mags]] and [[trevick]] meet Nessa; she reveals that the forest's trail markers have been changing unstably for two seasons and that she smelled a distant fire to the west in spring.
- S2.13–S2.15: Nessa guides the party into [[the-eldryn-forest]].
- S2.14: The forest's wren-token gift to [[trevick]] (the [[the-carved-wren]], with [[reinas-ring]] and [[the-owl-feather]]) is delivered here.
- S2.23: The cottage is found closed and abandoned — Nessa has sheltered at [[rhynharrans-hall]].
