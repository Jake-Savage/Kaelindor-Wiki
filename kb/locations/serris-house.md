## Overview
Serris's House is the [[greymere]] home of [[serris-dane]] and his parents, [[elric]] and [[berla]]. Serris keeps a private shrine here where he prays fervently, increasingly consumed by his recurring prophetic dreams.

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- [[serris-dane]] — the prophetic dreamer.
- [[elric]] and [[berla]] — Serris's parents; Elric carries a weather-foretelling lineage.
- Serris's private shrine.

## Events here
- S2.11: [[trevick]] (and, unseen, [[rell-aetris]]) follow Serris home.
- S2.14: [[mags]] and [[rell-aetris]] learn from Elric and Berla that Serris has suffered recurring prophetic monster-dreams for about three months — pre-dating the crisis — tied to his father's weather-foretelling lineage (see [[the-serris-mystery]]).

## DM notes
Serris's dreams began before the Yarrow attacks and are bound up with his father's weather-foretelling line; they are later revealed to be the Eldryn's prophetic warnings reaching the divided [[the-irdane]] succession (S2.21, S2.24).
