## Overview
The Bright Gate — "Thalasyr Eren," the Bright or Moonlight Gate — is an ancient sealed Feywild crossing within [[the-hallow-burn]]. It was sealed some 300 to 350 years ago, when the elves fled [[the-feywild]], and its wards anchor the workings that strengthen the planar boundary (see [[the-feywild-barrier]]). The origin of [[the-uthrel]] — ash marking where an infested tree once stood — sits right beside it.

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- The sealed gate itself and its ward-anchors.
- The uthrel's origin point — the ash where the infested tree stood — immediately beside the gate.

## Events here
- S2.22: [[caspian-talon]] and [[mags]] discover that the gate's wards were deliberately tampered with — altered to allow passage, then restored — by a stranger whose magic is foreign to [[the-eldryn]] (see [[who-tampered-the-gate]]). [[hesk]] convenes the Eldryn Great Council and posts a watch. [[rell-aetris]] shows an odd flash of excitement at the gate, which [[mags]] notes.

## DM notes
An outsider — later tied to the Furnace Brood (see [[the-furnace-brood]] and [[the-korrathian-conspiracy]]) — deliberately opened the gate to let [[the-uthrel]] through, then resealed it. [[rell-aetris]]'s reaction here is a seeded suspicion to follow up.
