## Overview
The Bright Gate — "Thalasyr Eren," the Bright or Moonlight Gate — is an ancient, hidden [[the-horizon-doors|Horizon Door]] within [[the-hallow-burn]] that opens onto [[the-feywild]]. It is **not** one of the three publicly-known Horizon Doors, but a forgotten one kept by [[the-eldryn]]. It was sealed some 300 to 350 years ago, when the elves fled [[the-feywild]], and its wards anchor the workings that strengthen the planar boundary (see [[the-feywild-barrier]]). The origin of [[the-uthrel]] — ash marking where an infested tree once stood — sits right beside it.

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- The sealed gate itself and its ward-anchors.
- The uthrel's origin point — the ash where the infested tree stood — immediately beside the gate.

## Events here
- S2.22: [[caspian-talon]] and [[mags]] discover that the gate's wards were deliberately tampered with — altered to allow passage, then restored — by a stranger whose magic is foreign to [[the-eldryn]] (see [[who-tampered-the-gate]]). [[hesk]] convenes the Eldryn Great Council and posts a watch. [[rell-aetris]] shows an odd flash of excitement at the gate, which [[mags]] notes.

## DM notes
An unknown outsider — a "stranger" whose magic is foreign to [[the-eldryn]] — deliberately opened the gate to let [[the-uthrel]] through, then resealed it; their identity is never established. The [[the-furnace-brood|Furnace Brood]] later came probing the incursion's trace (see [[the-korrathian-conspiracy]]) but are **not** established as the tamperer — per [[mags]]'s Speak with Dead, they do not know who caused it. [[rell-aetris]]'s odd reaction here is a seeded suspicion to follow up.
