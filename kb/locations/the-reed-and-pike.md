## Overview
The Reed and Pike is the inn at [[greymere]] where the party lodges and which becomes their base of operations throughout the Greymere arc. Its barkeep is [[harlem-pike]], who is more worried about the town's troubles than he lets on.

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- [[harlem-pike]] — the barkeep; outwardly steady but privately anxious about the crisis.

## Events here
- S2.10: The party arrives in Greymere, takes rooms, and gathers local gossip about the Yarrow attacks.
- S2.10 onward: The inn recurs as the party's Greymere base for the duration of the arc.
