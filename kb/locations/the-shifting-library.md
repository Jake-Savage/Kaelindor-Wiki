## Overview
The Shifting Library was a planar subspace created by the undead necromancer [[kiona-dumois]]. It travelled between planes — the Material Plane and the hells — and nested itself within real libraries (such as [[bridgemarch-library]]) so that her spectral figures could steal books. Kiona used it to search for the [[codex-mortis]], a necromantic tome whose accident had shattered her soul into undeath. The subspace collapsed in S2.09 when Kiona was destroyed.

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- [[kiona-dumois]] — the undead necromancer who created and ruled the subspace.
- Spectral book-thieves and reconstructable threats.
- An antechamber filled with blood-oozes.
- The great book-hall, patrolled by spectres with exploding cold-light lamps.
- A level of rune-bound exploding skeletons and floating skulls.
- A sacrificial chamber with a reconstructable portal to the hells, keyed to "blood of a being half in this world and half of another."
- Kiona's study.

## Events here
- S2.07: The party is pulled in and fights oozes and spectres.
- S2.08: They battle the exploding skeletons, sabotage the hell-portal runes ([[dakir]] channels their magic), and find two fresh sacrifices.
- S2.09: They destroy [[kiona-dumois]] (leaving no corpse), loot [[glasses-of-comprehend-languages]], [[brooch-of-shield]], and Kiona's [[codex-mortis]] notes (taken by [[caspian-talon]]), and escape as the subspace collapses (see [[the-shifting-library|the quest]]).

## DM notes
The portal in the sacrificial chamber leads to the hells, not the Feywild. Its key — "blood of a being half in this world and half of another" — and the [[codex-mortis]] notes carried off by [[caspian-talon]] are open threads.
