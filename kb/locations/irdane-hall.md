## Overview
Irdane Hall is the seat of Lord [[mathis-irdane]], the new lord of [[greymere]], who is in his mid-twenties. It is from here that Mathis governs the town and receives the party during the Yarrow crisis.

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- Lord [[mathis-irdane]] — the young lord of Greymere; later revealed as joint bearer of the [[the-irdane]] role.
- [[skarris]] — Mathis's wife.

## Events here
- S2.11: Mathis briefs the party on the Yarrows' attacks, the missing hedge-witch [[illyra-fen]], and her fatal "safety" charm; the Greenheart [[thalos-briarwood]] counsels only patience.
- S2.24: The party reconciles Mathis and [[serris-dane]] as joint bearers of the [[the-irdane]] role, resolving why the Eldryn's prophetic warnings had reached the wrong Irdane.

## DM notes
Mathis is revealed to be joint "Irdane" alongside [[serris-dane]] (see [[the-irdane]]): the hereditary interpreter line diverged through succession, so the Eldryn's prophetic warnings reached Serris rather than the sitting lord. His wife is [[skarris]].
