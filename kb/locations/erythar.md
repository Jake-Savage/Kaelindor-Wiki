## Overview
Erythar is the single larger kingdom of [[valenya]], sitting on the southern coast and recognised as the principal centre of power. Its capital is [[crownspire]]. Erythar leads the Erythian League — the defensive alliance formed roughly ninety years ago by the kingdoms west of the Stone March against Thal'Mirrith (see [[the-bleeding-years]]). The other nations tithe men or resources to the cause, and the best of everything is sent to Crownspire for the war effort.

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- [[crownspire]] — the capital, hub of the war effort, and home to one of the three [[the-horizon-doors]].
- The Erythian League — the war alliance Erythar heads, which [[kaelindor]] supports through tithes of gold and magical items.

## Events here
- Erythar has loomed over the campaign's politics from the start but the party has never travelled there directly; its capital Crownspire is the destination of the airship route out of Bridgemarch (S2.05).

## DM notes
[[lord-torston]]'s treason runs through Erythar: he is selling Kaelindor's war-tithe records and other state secrets to a buyer in Crownspire (see [[expose-torston]], [[the-korrathian-conspiracy]]). The Furnace Brood pays its agents via a Crownspire bank, tying Erythar's capital to the conspiracy thread.
