## Overview
Velton was a small mountain village in [[kaelindor]], the home of [[trevick]]. Returning from
a long hunt roughly three years before the campaign's present, Trevick found it smouldering and
ransacked — his mother missing, the village abandoned, and an oily growth (later understood to
be [[the-uthrel]]) where it had not been before. By the time help arrived from the neighbouring
village of Nerrin, Velton looked as though it had been derelict for years rather than days, an
early sign of the Uthrel's unnatural acceleration of decay.

## Events here
- Pre-campaign: Velton destroyed; Trevick orphaned and set on the road with [[muriel]].
- S2.19: Trevick recounts Velton's fall to the firbolgs when the talk turns to the moss.

## DM notes
Velton is the personal root of Trevick's stake in the Uthrel mystery; the moss he saw there ties
the village's fate directly to [[source-of-the-uthrel]].
