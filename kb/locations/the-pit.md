## Overview
The Pit is a semi-legal underground fighting ring in [[bridgemarch]], run by [[deacon-hardmarrow]]. Lord [[edward-harrington]] patronised it and backed the dock-worker fighter [[benny-carter]], which made it a key stop in [[the-bathhouse-vampire]] investigation.

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- [[deacon-hardmarrow]] — runs the fighting ring.
- [[benny-carter]] — dock-worker fighter, Harrington's protégé.

## Events here
- S2.02: [[mags]] heals [[benny-carter]] and secretly aids him with Minor Illusion to win his fight. Befriending Benny, the party learns that [[edward-harrington]] left him something hidden in his study — a lead that points them to [[harringtons-townhouse]].
