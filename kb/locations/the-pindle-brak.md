## Overview
The Pindle Brak (heard "Pindlebrack") is a small, dingy gnome pub in the same poor quarter of
[[greywatch]] as the [[the-three-copper-hatch|Three Copper Hatch]]. Its name means roughly **"the
cotter's assistant"** — the lad who helps the main worker rather than the worker himself
([[fabian-perennius]] and [[mags]] both know the gnomish term). It is one of the few spaces in town
built to gnome scale, so humans must stoop; the regulars keep human-sized glasses on hand but are
mildly put out when tall folk intrude on one of their own spaces. There is a larger gnome
population in Greywatch than the party saw further south.

## Notable features
- **Three drinks:** ordinary beer; the **East Gate Sour**, an herbal spirit the gnomes sip; and an
  unnamed, very potent spirit that translates as **"the bad idea"** — licorice-scented and strongly
  perception-altering, brewed specially for [[shadows-meet|Shadows Meet]] and meant to be drunk on
  the festival night. Some gnomes spend the whole festival simply sitting in the pub drinking it
  together — their own way of honouring the day.

## Events here
- S2.29: While [[caspian-talon]] and [[rell-aetris|Rell]] keep watch on the hatch,
  [[mags]], [[fabian-perennius|Fabian]] and [[trevick]] spend the afternoon here. Fabian wins over
  the gnomes with gnomish songs and tales of his mentor; Trevick downs "the bad idea" and spends a
  while in a perception-altered state before [[mags]] sobers him with Lesser Restoration. The
  party gathers gossip on the festival, the Mirrowen's identity-mimics, and (fruitlessly) on
  dragons.
