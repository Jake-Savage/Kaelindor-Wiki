## Overview
The Tiefling Shop is a warded metalworking shop in [[bridgemarch]]'s artisan district, run by two long-resident tieflings — likely Korrath-born and posing as refugees for around nine years. They were the Korrathian contact named in [[krolls-map]] and Kroll's orders. The shop was raided and gutted in S2.26.

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- The two tiefling proprietors — likely Korrath-born agents posing as refugees.
- A concealed blood-and-ash shrine to the [[the-furnace-brood]], hidden behind the fireplace, with trapped chests.

## Events here
- S2.26: The party stakes out the shop; Captain [[captain-thoronby]]'s squad raids it. A ward-blast injures soldiers, and the two tieflings kill themselves with acid. Behind the fireplace, the party finds a repeatedly-used blood/ash shrine to the [[the-furnace-brood]] and trapped chests guarded by a necrotic shadow-tentacle illusion (see [[the-korrathian-conspiracy]]).

## DM notes
The shop was a contact point rather than the conspiracy's true target within the city. The cell's suicide leaves the full purpose of [[the-korrathian-conspiracy]] unresolved — a chapter cliffhanger.
