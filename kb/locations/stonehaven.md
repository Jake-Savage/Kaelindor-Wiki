## Overview
Stonehaven is the market town where the campaign opened (S1.01). It is ruled by [[lady-aldara]], a pragmatic patron whose son and heir is [[edric-vayne]]. The party first gathered at an inn here, drawn together by the innkeeper to seek out Aldara's work. Aldara's aim was to reopen the long-abandoned Nerrin platinum mine so she could avoid raising war taxes on her people ([[reopen-the-nerrin-mine]]).

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- **The Bored Weasel** — the inn run by [[lucas-grovener]], an ex-Nerrin miner and widower of Lorna; he gifted Trevick a magical crossbow ([[trevicks-crossbow]]).
- **[[vaughn]]** — a flower-seller in town and a child survivor of Nerrin's slow depopulation.
- **[[brynna-hammerfist]]** — the town blacksmith, who forged [[muriel]]'s armour.

## Events here
- S1.01: The party is hired by [[lady-aldara]] to investigate and reopen the [[nerrin]] mine.
- S1.02: The party reports back from Nerrin; Aldara asks them to return the [[duvesin-signet-ring]] to Bridgemarch.
- S1.04: The party settles loose ends in town, sells goods, and collects [[muriel]]'s finished armour from [[brynna-hammerfist]].

## DM notes
Stonehaven functions as the campaign's first hub. The Nerrin job is the thread that pulls the party toward the meenlock infestation and the first sighting of the moss that becomes [[the-uthrel]].
