## Overview
The Brass Tower is a clocktower in [[bridgemarch]] associated with the [[church-of-calvyr]] and run/fortified by the gnome ex-paladin [[bartholomew-crane]]. Beneath it lies [[luciens-lair]], accessed by setting the clock to 9:50 — the meaning of the "0950" note found in [[harringtons-townhouse]].

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- [[bartholomew-crane]] — gnome ex-paladin of Calvyr who runs and fortified the tower.
- [[brother-cuthbert]] — Calvyr priest who interrupts the party's search.
- [[luciens-lair]] — hidden beneath the tower.

## Events here
- S2.03: A frightened [[bartholomew-crane]] kills [[theodore]] (Caspian's familiar), then confesses that [[lord-torston]] is a traitor and that he himself is protecting the vampire [[lucien]].
- S2.05–S2.06: The party searches the now-empty lair below the tower, interrupted by [[brother-cuthbert]], Calvyr guards, and the city watch.

## DM notes
"0950" is the key: set the clock to 9:50 to open the lair beneath the tower.
