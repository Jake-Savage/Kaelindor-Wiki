## Overview
The Deep Gnome Forge is a steam-powered forge worked by deep (svirfneblin) gnomes who live near the surface. Its warden is [[eklash]]. The gnomes possess ten to twelve [[books-of-prophecy]].

## Notable inhabitants & sites
- **[[eklash]]** — the forge's warden.
- The steam-powered forge itself, and an automaton that leads visitors to the gnome elders.
- The gnomes' store of ten to twelve [[books-of-prophecy]].

## Events here
- S1.03: The party trades [[the-fallen-star]] — the gnomes' last "rock" needed to complete a sixty-year coming-of-age rite — for the prophecy books ([[deliver-the-star-to-the-gnomes]]); an automaton leads them to the elders. [[eklash]] recognises the moss ([[the-uthrel]]) sample but treats it as taboo, revealing the gnomes have "a similar problem" in the under-dark.

## DM notes
The deep gnomes' under-dark "problem" with the moss foreshadows the reach of the threat that becomes [[the-uthrel]]. The gnomes wrongly believe it cannot spread on the surface — a dangerous assumption.
