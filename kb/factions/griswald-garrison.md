## Overview

The Griswald Garrison is a gnome mercenary company that serves as the standing guard force of the [[tower-of-heaven]], retained by the [[order-of-the-tower-of-heaven|Order of the Tower of Heaven]]. Its captain, [[captain-maddock]], proved willing to act against a usurper once persuaded of the truth, making the garrison a pivotal ally in restoring the rightful High Mage.

## Structure / ranks

A mercenary band under a **Captain** ([[captain-maddock]]), garrisoned at the Tower. No deeper internal structure is recorded.

## Relationship to the party

- **S1.02:** Slain Order mages bore witness to the garrison's presence at the Tower.
- **S1.03:** The party won over **[[captain-maddock]]**, who smuggled them inside disguised in mage robes. The garrison helped expose [[pyarz]] and afterward proved [[shalfay]]'s survival to the crowd, confirming his restoration.

The Griswald Garrison was a decisive ally in the Tower of Heaven coup; the party has had no recorded dealings with them since.
