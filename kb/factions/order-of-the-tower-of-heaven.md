## Overview

The Order of the Tower of Heaven is an arcane order of mages — particularly diviners and "star-watchers" — based at the [[tower-of-heaven]]. Its High Mage [[shalfay]] researches prophecy and the heavens, and the Order's diviners proved able to read meaning from the [[the-fallen-star|fallen star]] and the prophetic [[books-of-prophecy]]. The Order's interest in omens connects it to the wider mystery of [[the-uthrel|the uthrel]].

## Structure / ranks

The Order is led by a **High Mage** (Shalfay), and members bear rank tattoos — their absence is how the usurper [[pyarz]] was unmasked as a fraud. The Order employs the gnome [[griswald-garrison|Griswald Garrison]] as its guard force at the Tower. Beyond this, the internal hierarchy is not detailed in the records.

## Relationship to the party

- **S1.02:** The party recovered the [[the-fallen-star|fallen star]]; a memory-web revealed High Mage [[shalfay]]'s vision and his orders to bring the star to the druid [[derwyth]] and uncover who plotted against him.
- **S1.03:** With [[captain-maddock]]'s aid, the party infiltrated the Tower, exposed the usurper [[pyarz]] (no rank tattoos, vague divinations), and freed the real Shalfay. Shalfay revealed the star's purpose: a trade to the deep gnomes for prophetic books.
- **S1.04:** At Shalfay's request the party recovered the runaway students [[isabella-falcrae]] and [[bran-oris]]; Shalfay rewarded them with magic items and used the prophetic books to divine on the uthrel ("a warning against contamination").
- **S2.23:** Caspian sent word to the Tower and learned the books the party had donated were "wrong" or off — a thread prompting a future visit.

The Order is an early patron and a standing source of divination, with an unresolved question hanging over the donated prophecy books.
