## Overview

The Eldryn are the **Prior** peoples — centaurs, goliaths, and firbolgs — who dwell in and guard the deep forest (the Bramweald) west of [[greymere]]. They are part of [[the-prior]] alongside the [[the-quickened|Quickened]] nature-spirits, and they have kept the secret of [[the-uthrel|the uthrel]] for centuries, containing past outbreaks by burning the forest and sealing the wild magic with wards.

The Eldryn are deeply wary of outsiders carrying their secrets, but [[trevick]] — bearer of a Rhynharran [[the-watcher|Watcher]] — became their chosen go-between, and the party ultimately won an alliance with them in the fight against the uthrel.

## Structure / ranks

The Eldryn are a people, not a clerical order, governed by a **Council** (parliament) that debates collectively — as when it argued over whether to burn the forest to contain the latest outbreak. [[hesk]] is a confirmed Council member and camp leader; [[ethis]] is a centaur captain serving as his military second. The forest [[the-eldryn-camp|encampment]] held 50-plus Eldryn in six to eight patrols, with an unusually high proportion of spellcasters. Firbolgs such as [[he-who-rests-where-the-path-narrows]] hold spiritual authority, interpreting signs like the Watcher.

## Relationship to the party

- **S2.18:** Centaurs intercepted the wounded party, cured [[mags]]'s petrification, and demanded an oath not to investigate the forest crisis; the party mostly swore truthfully, and [[fabian-perennius]] negotiated a warning sent to town. They were housed with the [[the-weathered|Weathered]] [[reina]] and [[elset]].
- **S2.19:** [[trevick]]'s [[the-carved-wren|wren-token]] won a warm firbolg welcome; the firbolgs revealed [[hawk]] is a [[the-watcher|Watcher]] watching Trevick's lineage.
- **S2.20:** The party shared the **Nameweave** festival and exchanged gifts — until Trevick mentioned the "black moss" of his ruined village and the camp erupted; [[hesk]] detained the party.
- **S2.21:** Under [[mags]]'s Zone of Truth and won by the party's honesty, Hesk shared the truth of the uthrel, taught its destruction methods, **released the party from their oath, and agreed to ally** and warn Greymere.
- **S2.22:** A joint patrol found the uthrel's origin at the tampered [[the-bright-gate|Bright Gate]]; Caspian proved the wards had been deliberately altered. Hesk agreed to convene the Council early and post a permanent watch.
- **S2.23–S2.24:** Centaurs escorted the party and the Weathered back toward Greymere; the messenger [[vasha]] was reunited with the party at [[rhynharrans-hall]].

The Eldryn are now the party's key allies against the uthrel and the gate-tampering conspiracy.
