## Overview

The Advardis are a sorcerer bloodline of [[crownspire]], a family in which innate magic runs through the line. They are a minor presence in the campaign, met through [[leonard-advardis]], a son of the family who — unusually for an Advardis — lacks the bloodline's magic and was reduced to petitioning at the [[tower-of-heaven]].

## Structure / ranks

A hereditary magical family rather than an organisation; no formal ranks are recorded.

## Relationship to the party

- **S1.03:** The party met **[[leonard-advardis]]** at the Tower of Heaven lodging house — a sorcerer of the Advardis bloodline who, despite his lineage, possessed no magic, an oddity for the family.

The party's contact with the Advardis is limited to this single encounter; the family's wider significance is unexplored.
