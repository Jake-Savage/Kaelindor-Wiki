## Overview

The Church of Naelos — proper name and common name both **The Covenant** — holds [[naelos]] as the god of bonds, hospitality, gratitude, and honest dealing. Its clergy witness and keep oaths, officiate the **Nameweave** festival of bonds and gifts, and tend the communal life of villages. Its houses of worship are **Hearth-Halls, Quiet-Hearths, and Hearthsteps** depending on size, known commonly as **Hearths** or **Threadhouses**.

The Covenant is the party's most personal church connection: the gnome cleric [[mags]] (full name Smagslys Mørktbarn) serves Naelos, and her nephew the paladin [[orin-njord]] shares the devotion. Mags is the party's diplomatic and oath-keeping anchor.

## Structure / ranks

Worship houses: **Hearths** / **Threadhouses** (Hearth-Halls, Quiet-Hearths, Hearthsteps by size).

Clerical ladder, lowest to highest:
- **Witness** — lay helper or acolyte who observes and records vows, agreements, and sacred acts.
- **Steward** (or **Threadkeeper**) — local village priest; ceremonies, record-keeping, daily spiritual life.
- **Hearthwarden** — oversees a full Hearth-Hall; officiates major rituals and maintains community bonds.
- **Oathkeeper** — senior figure, regional or overseeing multiple Hearth-Halls.

## Relationship to the party

- **S1.01:** [[trevick]] earned an unspoken blessing of Naelos by tending Lorna's grave at [[nerrin]].
- **S2.11–S2.13:** [[mags]] performed Naelos-flavoured rites in the [[greymere]] crisis — casting Water Walk to greet the lake spirits and making offerings to the troubled [[the-quickened|Quickened]].
- **S2.17:** Mags's selfless Lesser Restoration to save [[fabian-perennius]] from a mist-creature helped trigger Fabian's [[fabians-radiant-magic|radiant magic]]; Mags was herself petrified to wood the same session.
- **S2.18:** Cured by the centaurs, Mags helped the party swear an oath to the [[the-eldryn|Eldryn]] leader [[hesk]] — an oath of exactly the kind the Covenant exists to keep.
- **S2.20:** The party celebrated **Nameweave** with the Eldryn, exchanging gifts in keeping with Naelos's festival of bonds.
- **S2.24:** Mags cast Speak with Dead on [[hethan-kroll]]'s corpse at [[rhynharrans-hall]], extracting intelligence for the investigation.

Through Mags, the Covenant's values of honesty and kept oaths repeatedly anchor the party's dealings, especially with the Eldryn.
