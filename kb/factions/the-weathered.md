## Overview

The Weathered are a rank and an order within [[the-warding-of-rhynharran|Rhynharran's Ward]]: pilgrims who have spent long years living alone in the wilderness as hermits, often (though not always) druids. They sit high in the Warding's ladder, between the local Greenhearts and the singular Oak Heart, and are trusted to carry the church's most sensitive errands — such as messages to the secretive [[the-eldryn|Eldryn]].

In the [[greymere]] crisis, three Weathered were sent by the Greenheart [[thalos-briarwood]] as messengers to the Eldryn and went missing, prompting the party's forest mission.

## Structure / ranks

The Weathered are not a separate hierarchy but the fourth rung of the Warding's clerical ladder (Wildtender → Hornbearers → Greenhearts → **The Weathered** → The Oak Heart). They serve as solitary pilgrim-hermits and, when needed, as trusted emissaries.

## Relationship to the party

- **S2.15:** The Greenheart commissioned [[trevick]] to find the three missing Weathered messengers; the party recovered [[reina]]'s [[reinas-ring|Ring of Protection +1]] in the forest.
- **S2.18:** The two surviving Weathered, **[[reina]] and [[elset]]**, were found housed at the [[the-eldryn-camp|Eldryn camp]]; the third messenger had died. They hinted at the gravity of the crisis.
- **S2.19:** Reina explained the Eldryn's distrust of outsiders who learn the forest's secrets.
- **S2.23:** The party escorted Reina and Elset back toward Greymere under centaur guard and delivered them to [[rhynharrans-hall]].

The Weathered were the object of the party's first Eldryn-facing mission and a source of insight into the Eldryn's caution.
