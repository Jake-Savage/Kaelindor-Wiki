## Overview

The Furnace Brood is a brutal [[korrathian|Korrathian]] faction operating covertly on the Material Plane, led by a figure styled the **Broodfather**. Its ethos of fire and unmaking aligns it with the cult of [[vraxyn]] ([[the-ruin-of-vraxyn|the Ruin]]), and its operations are marked by concealment magic and compulsion. The Brood is tied to *investigating and probing* the [[the-uthrel|uthrel]] incursion at the [[the-bright-gate|Bright Gate]] — its agent [[hethan-kroll]] was sent to find whether the incursion "left a trace" — making it a central antagonist of the [[the-korrathian-conspiracy|Korrathian conspiracy]] arc. It is **not** established as the cause of the incursion: per [[mags]]'s Speak with Dead on Kroll, the Brood do not know who tampered with the gate or why ("I do not know. No one knows").

## Structure / ranks

Led by an unseen **Broodfather**, the Brood grants magic and Crownspire-funded pay to agents like [[hethan-kroll]] via Korrathian contracts (see [[krolls-korrathian-contract]]). It runs cells under deep concealment — such as the long-resident tieflings of the Bridgemarch [[the-tiefling-shop|metalworking shop]] — who use blood-and-ash shrines and die rather than be taken. The deeper hierarchy is unknown.

## Relationship to the party

- **S2.14:** The Brood agent [[hethan-kroll]] and his band arrived in [[greymere]] offering to "drive back" the Quickened; [[mathis-irdane]] refused, and [[mags]] seeded rumours they were scammers.
- **S2.18 / S2.22:** The Eldryn warned the town against outsider intervention; the party's patrol found the deliberately tampered Bright Gate.
- **S2.23:** Kroll was found ritually murdered (single blow, untouched tent); the party recovered his locked box.
- **S2.24:** The box revealed the Brood's sigil, charm/compulsion scrolls, a flawed [[amulet-of-proof-against-detection]], a [[krolls-map|marked map]] of incursion sites, and the [[krolls-korrathian-contract|Korrathian contract]]. [[rell-aetris]] recognised the Furnace Brood; Mags's Speak with Dead found Kroll's goal was to slip into the wood and "find if the incursion left a trace."
- **S2.26:** [[captain-thoronby]]'s raid on the Bridgemarch cell found a **blood-and-ash shrine to the Furnace Brood**; the tieflings killed themselves. The conspiracy's full purpose remains unresolved, and [[oscar-wicklow]] kept the marked map.

The Furnace Brood is the party's principal active enemy, tied to *investigating* the uthrel incursion (sent to find its trace) and to the unresolved Korrathian conspiracy — but they are not established as the cause of the incursion, and per Speak with Dead the Brood do not know who caused it.
