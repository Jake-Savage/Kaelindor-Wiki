## Overview

Kroll's Mercenaries are a sellsword band that served as the public face of [[hethan-kroll]]'s mission in [[greymere]]. Unknown to the town, the band was held in **magical loyalty** to Kroll by compulsion — a binding that began to fade after his death. Captain [[hethan-kroll]] was a secret [[the-furnace-brood|Furnace Brood]] agent; with him dead, his lieutenant [[annet-brigg]] took command of the now-leaderless band.

## Structure / ranks

A mercenary company under a captain. [[hethan-kroll]] led until his murder; [[annet-brigg]] succeeded him. The rank and file were bound to Kroll by compulsion magic rather than ordinary loyalty, leaving them adrift once that magic wore off.

## Relationship to the party

- **S2.14:** Kroll's band offered to drive back the Quickened for coin; [[mathis-irdane]] refused and [[mags]] spread word they were scammers.
- **S2.23:** Kroll was found murdered, his band still magically loyal to him; the messenger [[vasha]] had faced them down. [[dakir]] took Kroll's records and locked box.
- **S2.24:** Clairvoyance confirmed the band's compulsion loyalty was wearing off after Kroll's death.
- **S2.25:** [[fabian-perennius]] negotiated with the new leader **[[annet-brigg]]**: the band returned 406 gold extorted from the town in exchange for a writ to Quintus Palaveus of [[crownspire]] plus 200 gold expenses, then departed south, paying respects to Kroll's body.

The party defused the mercenary band peacefully, removing them from the Greymere crisis without bloodshed.
