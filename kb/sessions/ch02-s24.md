## Recap
Back at the Hall, Rell finally opens Kroll's box, revealing 11 spellscrolls (charm/compulsion magic), an Amulet of Proof against Detection (with a built-in flaw), a Furnace Brood sigil, and two documents: a crudely drawn map marking sites in the Mirrowen, the Branweald (Caerweth), the Stonemarch (near Trevick's village), eastern Marivane, and the forest fire site; and a Korrathian (Infernal) "contract" granting the magically untalented Kroll Korrathian magic and pay (to a Crownspire bank) from the Furnace Brood's "Broodfather." Rell recognises the Furnace Brood as a brutal Korrathian faction. Clairvoyance shows the mercenaries still magically loyal to dead Kroll, the compulsion wearing off.

The party reconciles Mathis and Serris: explaining the Eldryn's divergent succession, they persuade Mathis he and Serris Dane together are the Irdane and must work as a team. Mathis, relieved, resolves to summon Serris. Speaking with the centaur Vasha, then casting Speak with Dead on Kroll's corpse (Mags disguised as a Furnace Brood member), they learn Kroll's objective was to create disturbance so he could slip unnoticed into the wood to "find out if the incursion left a trace" — and that neither Kroll nor anyone knows the incursion's purpose. His next orders would come from a contact in Bridgemarch (address given). Kroll's killing has the marks of an Itharian death-ritual; Mathis is glad of it but disclaims ordering it.

## Key facts learned
- Kroll's box: charm/compulsion spellscrolls, an (intentionally flawed) Amulet of Proof against Detection, and a Korrathian "contract" with the Furnace Brood's "Broodfather," paid via a Crownspire bank.
- A map marks uthrel/incursion-related sites: Mirrowen (x2), Branweald/Caerweth, Stonemarch (near Trevick's village), eastern Marivane, and the forest fire site.
- Kroll's mission: create disturbance to slip into the wood and check whether "the incursion left a trace"; the incursion's purpose is unknown even to him.
- Kroll's next orders would come from a contact in Bridgemarch (address obtained).
- Mathis and Serris Dane are jointly the Irdane; they will work together going forward.
- Kroll's murder bears the marks of an Itharian crossing-ritual (untouched surroundings).

## Threads opened / advanced
- The Furnace Brood are revealed to be *probing* the incursion (Kroll was sent to find its trace) — not established as its cause; who tampered with the gate remains unknown.
- A Bridgemarch contact address becomes the party's next lead.
- The Serris/Irdane succession is resolved in Greymere.
