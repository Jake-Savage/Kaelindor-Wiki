## Recap
Following up on missing library books that may connect to the vampire scare, the party investigates an old library and is engulfed by an unnatural patch of shadow that transports them into a planar subspace. In an antechamber, Dakir opens a decanter and blood-borne oozes attack; the party destroys them.

Beyond, they enter a vast hall of books tended by spectral figures, with exploding cold-light lamps that detonate as combat erupts. The party fights through waves of spectres and light-creatures. They learn the library belongs to Kiona Dumois, who pursued necromancy via a book called the Codex Mortis; an accident shattered her soul and turned her into a powerful undead. This subspace, "The Shifting Library," travels between planes and nests in real libraries so her spectral figures can steal books — across both the Material Plane and the hells — in her search to recover the Codex Mortis. They recover a Speak with Dead scroll, infernal notes, and clear more rooms of spectral figures, finding additional spellscrolls and a diamond.

## Key facts learned
- The Shifting Library is a planar subspace created by the undead necromancer Kiona Dumois.
- It travels between planes (Material Plane and the hells) and nests in existing libraries to steal books.
- Kiona's soul was shattered by an accident involving the Codex Mortis, a tome on reanimating the dead; she seeks to recover the lost book.
- The library is staffed by spectral figures created by Kiona to do the work.
