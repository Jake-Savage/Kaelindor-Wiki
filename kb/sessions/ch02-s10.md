## Recap
Over the following weeks the vampire hunt winds down with no slain vampire to claim. The party's fame grows through Mags and Fabian; the Church of Calvyr takes a reputational hit; Benny's claim on Harrington's title becomes accepted, with Torston publicly burying the hatchet, and Damar slowly recovers with Lucien gone. Dakir takes Bartholomew's components to Talan Pryce at the Church of Calvyr, who finishes the spell-storing device (it can now hold low- to mid-level spells). Rell returns from private business. Trevick receives a troubling note (later tied to "Joster") and reveals magical crackers; Fabian receives an owlbear plushie ("Beakers") for Nameweave. The party takes interest in a new job in Greymere.

The town of Greymere, northwest of Bridgemarch on the lake, depends on fishing now disrupted by "water spirits"; two men have died. Chancellor Oscar Wicklow personally briefs the party, explaining "the Prior": the Quickened (place-bound nature spirits, mostly benign) and the Eldryn (sentient species — centaurs, goliaths, firbolgs). The boundary is a chain of islands (the Thal Stones); the Quickened have turned aggressive. Reward is official favour plus 1400 gold. The party (collecting Muriel en route) travels three days along the Crossway and reaches Greymere, staying at The Reed and Pike. They begin gathering gossip: Harlem Pike the barkeep is more worried than he lets on, and Rell's Detect Thoughts flags an oddly excited man near the temple of Rhynharran.

## Key facts learned
- The vampire scare fades; Benny's inheritance succeeds; Damar recovers; the party's fame grows.
- Dakir's spell-storing device (from Bartholomew, finished by Talan Pryce) now stores low- to mid-level spells.
- Greymere depends on fishing, now disrupted by aggressive Quickened; two fishermen killed; reward = official favour + 1400 gold.
- "The Prior": the Quickened (place-bound spirits) and the Eldryn (centaurs, goliaths, firbolgs). Boundary = the Thal Stones.
- Chancellor Oscar Wicklow (rumoured secret priest of Itharis) treats the mission as a test of the party.
- A man near the temple of Rhynharran shows abnormal excitement about the crisis.
