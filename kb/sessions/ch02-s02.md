## Recap
At The Floodgate, Fabian works the patrons while Mags mingles with the regulars. They learn Harrington patronised a semi-legal fighting ring, "The Pit," run by Deacon Hardmarrow, and favoured a fighter named Benny Carter who works the docks. When Nelly Fairchild arrives that evening, Mags calms her nerves and Rell covertly reads her thoughts. Fabian charms Nelly with the pretext of arranging a memorial ball for Harrington.

Nelly and her companion Tess reveal Harrington was loyal, ambitious, and disliked by Torston over something personal that grew over the past year. Harrington was about to gain the upper hand using information he intended to bring to Princess Órlaith, Commander of the Bridgeguard. They also describe the unknown beautiful woman who left with Harrington that night — whom both Caspian and Fabian recognise as Elaine, the woman they saw in Lord Torston's office.

The party visits The Pit. Mags heals Benny before a fight and secretly aids him with Minor Illusion so he wins. Befriending him, they learn Harrington had told Benny that, if anything happened to him, Benny was to receive something left in his study. Benny leads Rell and Dakir into Harrington's guarded townhouse. In the study they find ciphered letters, a cipher disk, a hidden compartment with more papers (including one addressed to "Benedict"), and a note reading "0950." Rell collects everything and they slip back out.

## Key facts learned
- Harrington ran the underground fighting ring The Pit (run by Deacon Hardmarrow) and backed the fighter Benny Carter.
- Harrington possessed information he meant to take to Princess Órlaith, Commander of the Bridgeguard, to gain advantage over Torston.
- The woman who left with Harrington was Elaine — the same woman seen in Torston's office.
- Harrington left Benny something hidden in his study and feared a spy had been watching him.
- Recovered from the study: ciphered letters, a cipher disk, a sealed letter for "Benedict," and a note reading "0950."

## Threads opened / advanced
- Suspicion shifts toward Torston himself and his associate Elaine.
- Benny Carter is established as Harrington's secret protégé and a recurring ally.
- The ciphered documents and "0950" become the key to unravelling Harrington's scheme.
