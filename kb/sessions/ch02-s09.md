## Recap
In a long, attrition-heavy battle, Kiona summons waves of undead scholars and absorbs them to heal, draining Fabian repeatedly and casting a portal spell anchored to the Material Plane. The party grinds her down — Trevick's arrows finally strike her in the face and kill her; she leaves no corpse and the undead return to their work. As the library destabilises, the party loots her study (potions, ruby dust, Glasses of Comprehend Languages, a Broach of Shield, and Kiona's dense infernal notes on the Codex Mortis for Caspian) and opens their own portal back to the Material Plane, emerging in the original library as the subspace tears apart.

Back home, the party reports vaguely to the priests. Reviewing Trevick's stolen study papers, they conclude Torston has been communicating with people of consequence in Crownspire — enough to raise suspicion but not to prove treason outright. They decide to let the Torston matter lie for now, keep an eye on Benny, and look for work around Bridgemarch. Fabian must reluctantly return the sunblade, and he and Caspian begin scheming to get it back one day.

## Key facts learned
- Kiona Dumois is destroyed; the Shifting Library collapses.
- Caspian acquires Kiona's notes on the Codex Mortis (dangerous to read, but reveal secrets over time).
- Trevick's stolen papers show Torston corresponding with prominent figures in Crownspire — suspicious but not conclusive.

## Threads opened / advanced
- The Shifting Library quest is resolved.
- The Torston-treason thread is deliberately shelved (kept "on the lookout"), with Benny watched.
- Fabian returns the sunblade; a future plan to reclaim it is seeded.
