## Recap
While searching Lucien's lair, the party is interrupted by Brother Cuthbert, Church of Calvyr guards, and city watchmen; they leave with their details taken. To confirm the swiped clothing is Damar's, they visit the tailor Merric Linvale, who recognises the garment as made on Lord Torston's account for Damar.

At the Torston estate, the party reports finding the lair under the clocktower. Caspian's Detect Thoughts shows Torston is surprised, defensive, and fiercely protective of anything concerning Damar — but warming to using the party. Mags offers divine protection against the vampire, and Torston privately admits a member of his household has been affected. He leads them to Damar, who is ill and resigned. Mags casts Calm Emotions, briefly freeing Damar from Lucien's charm; he breaks down, and Torston embraces him.

While Caspian probes Damar's mind, Damar confesses he wanted Lucien to return, gave the vampire papers over weeks, and reveals: Torston's regular courier is Elaine Blackthorne; Damar does not believe Torston is a traitor; Torston is interested in the Queen's circle for unknown reasons and works on his papers in his private office. Meanwhile Trevick, escorted by a guard to "test the wards," breaks into Torston's private study and pockets concealed papers. The party declines to stay the night and heads home before the planned vampire hunt.

## Key facts learned
- The recovered clothing is confirmed Damar's (via tailor Merric Linvale, on Torston's account).
- Damar was charmed by Lucien for ~3 months and willingly passed Torston's papers to the vampire.
- Torston's courier is Elaine Blackthorne; Torston works his secret papers in his private office and is fixated on the Queen's circle.
- Calm Emotions can give a charm victim a window of clarity; chronic charm wears off slowly over time.
- Trevick secretly extracted concealed papers from Torston's private study.

## Threads opened / advanced
- Damar's charm and recovery becomes its own thread; Torston is shown as a man genuinely protecting his husband.
- Elaine Blackthorne confirmed as the courier link in Torston's network.
- Trevick's stolen study papers (later read) point to Torston's Crownspire contacts.
