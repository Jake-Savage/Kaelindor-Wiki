## Recap
Climbing to the next level, the party fights rune-controlled skeletons and Mage Hand-wielding floating skulls amid the stacks. The skeletons explode when destroyed, and the fight is gruelling — Mags is briefly knocked unconscious before Fabian revives her. They recover spellscrolls and treasure.

Solving an infernal riddle ("the blood of a being half in this world and half of another"), they unlock doors using blood — Trevick's fails, but Mags's bone shard works. Behind one door they find two recently sacrificed bodies in a chamber holding a reconstructable portal to the hells. Caspian sabotages the runes to make it harder to reopen, and Dakir channels some of the runes' magic to restore his power. Mags offers prayers for the corrupted dead. The party finds more books and scrolls, then opens the final door to discover the spectral apparition of Kiona Dumois bent over her desk, who asks what they've brought her.

## Key facts learned
- The library houses a reconstructable portal to the hells, fuelled by sacrifice; Caspian learns how to sabotage its runes.
- Two fresh sacrificial bodies were found, too magically corrupted for Mags to help.
- Doors are keyed to the blood of a being "half in this world and half of another" — Mags's undead-tinged bone shard works.
- Kiona Dumois exists as a powerful spectral undead presiding over the library.

## Threads opened / advanced
- The hell portal is identified and partially sabotaged.
- The confrontation with Kiona Dumois is set up for the next session.
