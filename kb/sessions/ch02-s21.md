## Recap
Believing the party's arrival no coincidence, Hesk interrogates them about the uthrel. Won over by their honesty (and Mags's sincere Zone of Truth), she relents and tells the truth: the "moss" is the uthrel, a Feywild scourge dangerous to all living things, spreading wherever there is magic; it drove the elves from their homeland and is feared as a weapon in the great eastern war. The Eldryn contained an outbreak deep in the forest by burning it and sealing every channel of magic with wards — which is why the forest, and the lake, are starving and the Quickened turned hostile. The uthrel entered the Feywild ~900 years ago and is now returning to the Material Plane carried by travellers; travel to the Feywild is no longer possible.

The Eldryn teach the party to destroy uthrel: isolate it with wards to cut ambient magic, destroy it with magical fire, burn its physical fuel with mundane fire, avoid the gas it releases, and check for spread. Discussing Serris, they realise his dreams are the Eldryn's warnings reaching the wrong "Irdane": succession diverged, so both Mathis and Serris (Serris Dane) carry pieces of the bloodline. Hesk, now a confirmed Council member, releases the party from their vow, agrees to ally and send word, and the party plans to join patrols to learn uthrel-handling before returning to Greymere.

## Key facts learned
- The uthrel is a magic-devouring scourge from the Feywild (entered it ~900 years ago), dangerous to all life, that drove the elves out and is feared as a war weapon; it is not alive and hard to detect.
- The Eldryn contained it by burning the forest and warding off all ambient magic — causing the starving forest, starving lake, and hostile Quickened.
- The uthrel returns to the Material Plane carried over crossings by travellers; Feywild travel is no longer possible.
- How to destroy uthrel: ward off ambient magic, magical fire, mundane fire on its fuel, avoid its gas, check for spread.
- Serris's dreams are the Eldryn's warnings; the "Irdane" line diverged, so Mathis and Serris (Serris Dane) together hold the role.
