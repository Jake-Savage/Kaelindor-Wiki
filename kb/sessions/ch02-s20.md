## Recap
On Nameweave (a festival of bonds, gifts, and gratitude associated with Naelos), Fabian cooks breakfast and the party exchanges plush gifts and fond memories — revealing personal history (Fabian as a reluctant seventh-son bard; Dakir's long search for belonging; Trevick's three years alone since his village was lost). Rell privately shares his Horizonborn nature with Fabian, removing his mask to commune wordlessly. Mags hosts a Nameweave gathering, secretly casting Zone of Truth over it.

Hawk and Theodore scout a patrol to a "balled" burnt zone ~1km across, ringed by wards, magic-starved, scattered with animal and even pig bones — confirming it was not a purely magical fire. Fabian's new tragic firbolg ballad ("he who warned of the storm," etc.) clearly strikes raw nerves about a recent contentious decision to burn part of the forest; through cold-reading and Rell's mind-reading, he learns the Eldryn debated in a "parliament" whether to burn, with some arguing the perimeter was too large and the Quickened are starving as a result. When Trevick tells the firbolgs about the "black moss" that destroyed his village, the camp erupts in alarm. Hesk and Ethis break up the party. Everyone hears the word "uthrel," and Caspian confirms the party has encountered the black moss before.

## Key facts learned
- The burnt zone (~1km across) was deliberately created, magic-starved, and warded; contains animal and pig bones — not a purely magical fire.
- The Eldryn deliberated and chose to burn part of the forest; the Quickened are starving because the burn/wards cut off ambient magic.
- The destructive "black moss" Trevick survived is known to the Eldryn as the uthrel and provokes deep fear.
- Nameweave is a festival of bonds/gratitude tied to Naelos; the Eldryn don't observe it.
- Rell is "Horizonborn" and communes wordlessly through his unmasked, many-eyed face.

## Threads opened / advanced
- The uthrel is named and explicitly linked to Trevick's destroyed village and the Greymere crisis.
- The Eldryn's secret (burning the forest to contain the uthrel) is exposed; the party is detained.
- Fabian/Rell and Dakir/party bonds deepen; Rell's Horizonborn nature revealed to Fabian.
