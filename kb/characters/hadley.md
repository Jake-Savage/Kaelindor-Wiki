## Overview
Hadley is the ferryman to the [[tower-of-heaven]]. He tested the party with [[shalfay]]'s passphrase ("Fair is the day... fairer by far the jewels of the evening"), then revealed that Shalfay was presumed dead, that [[pyarz]] now acted as High Mage, and that the Tower's divinations had grown vague and riddling — laying out its layout and the [[griswald-garrison]] guards (S1.03).

## Appearances
- S1.03: Tests the party, then briefs them on the situation at the Tower.
