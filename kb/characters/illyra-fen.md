## Overview
Illyra Fen is an elf-blooded hedge-witch of [[greymere]] who made the "safety" charm given to the drowned fisherman Bran Hosteigg — the charm around which the [[the-quickened|Yarrows']] killings began (S2.11). She vanished before the party arrived; orders exist to detain her if she returns. The party finds her hiding in a concealed dwelling deep in the [[the-eldryn-forest]] (her haven, S2.15).

## Knowledge & motivations
Illyra fled not from guilt but from fear: with elven blood, she expected to be scapegoated for her heritage rather than judged on her actions. Crucially, she reveals **how her charms work** — by skimming "little streams of magic" from the wild, the same ambient magic the Yarrows feed on — and that **this ambient magic has been drying up for months**, growing harder to harvest. This lets [[caspian-talon]] connect the fading magic to a thinning [[the-feywild-barrier|Feywild barrier]], a major clue toward the uthrel. The party leaves her in peace, exonerated in their eyes; her charm was incidental, not the true cause.
