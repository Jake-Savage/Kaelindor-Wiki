## Overview
Jory Pike is a scavenger of [[toadhop]], suspected (with [[todd-macby]]) of the grave robbery but proven innocent. Terrified, he described five or six small green clawed creatures digging up [[frederick-dartwell]]'s grave and vanishing — the clue that let the party deduce quasits and [[joster-sirit]] (S1.05).

- S1.05: Cleared as a suspect; his account of the quasits cracks the case.
