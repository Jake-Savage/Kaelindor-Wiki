## Overview
Amir Merillac is named in one of three Furnace Brood instruction-scrolls the party recovered (S2.27). Per the scroll, he is a noble at a manor near the town of Fairfield in [[offendale|Offendale]] (reached south by water) who has been doing work for the [[the-furnace-brood|Furnace Brood]], but whom the Brood has come to suspect. [[hethan-kroll|Hethan Kroll]] was to travel there with his best people as security and to watch for the noble double-crossing the Brood — an order Kroll never carried out (he was killed in S2.23). The party has not met Merillac; all of this is from the scroll.

## Appearances
- S2.27: Named in the first recovered Furnace Brood scroll (Kroll's orders).
