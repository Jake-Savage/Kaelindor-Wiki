## Overview
Valeria is a sister of [[fabian-perennius|Fabian]], mentioned (not yet met) in S2.28. According to Fabian, she is obsessed with the weird, aberration-like creatures of [[the-mirrowen|the Mirrowen]] and the scholarly debate around them, forever going on about the "aberration-y" specimens kept in the museum. Fabian expects his family — Valeria especially — to be intensely jealous that he has reached the Mirrowen first, and hopes the party might meet her there.

## Appearances
- S2.28: Named by Fabian as a possible Greywatch contact and an aberration-scholar; not yet encountered.

## DM notes
Mentioned-only. Surname not stated; recorded under her given name to avoid asserting a family name (Fabian's brothers carry varying names — Marcellus, and Quintus Palaveus). Whether she is actually in Greywatch is unconfirmed.
