## Overview
Damar is the husband of [[lord-torston]], who fiercely protects him. Charmed by the vampire [[lucien]] for around three months, Damar willingly passed Torston's papers to the vampire over weeks (S2.05–06). Mags's Calm Emotions briefly frees him from the charm, prompting a tearful breakdown and Torston's embrace.

## Knowledge & motivations
Under and after the charm, Damar wanted Lucien to return; he does **not** believe Torston is a traitor, and revealed that Torston's courier is [[elaine-blackthorne]] and that Torston is fixated on the Queen's circle (S2.06). He recovers slowly once Lucien leaves the city.

## Appearances
- S2.05: His clothing is recovered from Lucien's lair.
- S2.06: Briefly freed by Calm Emotions; confesses the leak and names Elaine as courier.
- S2.10: Slowly recovers with Lucien gone (the `free-damar` thread resolves).
