## Overview
Nelly Fairchild works at [[the-floodgate]] pub and regularly accompanied [[edward-harrington]] to the [[silver-moon-bathhouse]] (S2.01). Charmed by [[fabian-perennius]] (with the pretext of a memorial ball) and covertly mind-read by [[rell-aetris]], she and her companion Tess revealed that Harrington was loyal and ambitious, disliked by [[lord-torston]] over a growing personal grievance, about to gain the upper hand using information meant for Princess [[princess-orlaith|Órlaith]], and that the unknown beautiful woman who left with him was [[elaine-blackthorne]] (S2.02).

## Appearances
- S2.01: Established as Harrington's bathhouse companion; lead to The Floodgate.
- S2.02: Gives the party intelligence on Harrington's scheme, the deaths, and Elaine; leads toward The Pit.
