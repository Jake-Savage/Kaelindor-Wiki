## Overview
Hethan Kroll is an outsider mercenary captain who arrives in [[greymere]] with his band, the [[krolls-mercenaries]], offering to "drive back" the [[the-quickened|Quickened]] for coin (S2.14) — an offer [[mathis-irdane]] refuses. His mercenaries are bound to him by magic, remaining loyal even after his death. He is found murdered in his camp in the dead of night, struck down by a single blow, his tent left untouched and unplundered — the marks of an [[itharis|Itharian]] crossing-ritual (S2.23–24).

## Knowledge & motivations
Kroll's mercenary front concealed his true role as a **secret agent of the [[the-furnace-brood]]**, a brutal [[korrathian]] faction. A locked box in his tent yields the truth (S2.24): charm/compulsion spellscrolls, a deliberately-flawed [[amulet-of-proof-against-detection]], a Furnace Brood sigil, [[krolls-map]] marking incursion sites (including the Stonemarch near [[trevick]]'s village, Velton), and a [[krolls-korrathian-contract|Korrathian contract]] granting the magically-untalented Kroll Korrathian magic and pay from the Brood's "Broodfather" via a Crownspire bank.

Mags's Speak with Dead reveals his objective: to **create a disturbance so he could slip unnoticed into the wood and check whether "the incursion left a trace"** — but neither Kroll nor anyone he knew understood the incursion's purpose. His next orders would have come from a contact in [[bridgemarch]] (address obtained).

## Appearances

## DM notes / secrets
- **Who killed Kroll is unresolved.** The Itharian death-ritual marks, [[mathis-irdane]]'s relief (while disclaiming the order), and the vanished centaur messenger [[vasha]] are the clues; [[thalos-briarwood]] is suspected of knowing.
- Kroll is the first concrete link from the Greymere uthrel incursion to the [[the-furnace-brood]] / [[the-korrathian-conspiracy|Korrathian conspiracy]] — and his map ties the gate tampering to a wider campaign.
