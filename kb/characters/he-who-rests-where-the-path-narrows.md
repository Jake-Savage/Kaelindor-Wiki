## Overview
He Who Rests Where the Path Narrows is a firbolg of the [[the-eldryn]] who welcomes [[trevick]] warmly when shown the [[the-carved-wren|wren-token]] and explains the significance of [[hawk]] as a [[the-watcher|Watcher]] — a spirit [[rhynharran]] sends to watch a chosen lineage across generations (S2.19–20).

## Appearances
- S2.19: Reveres Hawk as a Watcher and explains its meaning to Trevick.
- S2.20: Among the firbolgs at Nameweave when Trevick's tale of the moss alarms the camp.
