## Overview
Ivo Pierron (also styled **Sir Ivo**) holds the title of **Warden of the Beacon** (also heard
"Beacon Keeper"), the elected ruler of [[greywatch]] and, through it, [[the-mirrowen|the
Mirrowen]]. The Warden is elected from Greywatch's **council** for a **twelve-year term**; Ivo
is in his **last year**, with roughly eight months left, which has the town speculating about his
successor. He is **close to [[lord-valcarin|Lord Valcarin]]**, the ruler of [[trevaryn|Trevaryn]],
and so will have influence over who is chosen.

## Knowledge & motivations
Known only at second hand, from townsfolk gossip (S2.29). His successor must come from Greywatch's
council and hold Valcarin's approval; only two or three council members are credible candidates.

## Appearances
- S2.29: Named in town gossip as the outgoing Warden of the Beacon while the party gathers
  information in [[greywatch]]. Not met in person.
