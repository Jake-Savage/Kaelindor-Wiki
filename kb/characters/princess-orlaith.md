## Overview
Princess Órlaith is the Commander of the Bridgeguard and heir of [[kaelindor]]. [[edward-harrington]] sought her favour, intending to bring her the information that would let him gain the upper hand over [[lord-torston]] (S2.02) — the treason case that got him killed.

## Appearances
- S2.02: Named as the figure Harrington meant to approach with his evidence.

## DM notes / secrets
- Harrington's intended channel for exposing [[lord-torston]]'s treason; the case never reached her, leaving the `expose-torston` thread open.
