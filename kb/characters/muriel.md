## Overview
Muriel is the juvenile owlbear companion of [[trevick]] (referred to as "Muriel 2"), serving as the party's tank. The blacksmith [[brynna-hammerfist]] forged her custom armour and horn-caps (S1.02), and she wears the [[necklace-of-adaptation]] passed to her by Trevick. When Velton (Trevick's village) fell, it was Muriel who growled and nipped Trevick back from the ruins, saving his life.

## Knowledge & motivations
A loyal beast companion who acts on Trevick's bond and direction.

## Appearances
- S1.01–02: Trevick's companion from the founding; gains armour from [[brynna-hammerfist]].
- S2.10: Collected by the party en route to Greymere.
- S2.15: Left behind for the deep forest trek — fortunately, given the far larger giant owlbear the party encountered.
