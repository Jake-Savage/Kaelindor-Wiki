## Overview
Anwen Ireth is a druid of [[greymere]] (aligned with [[the-warding-of-rhynharran]]) who communes with the [[the-quickened|Quickened]], assisted by her novice [[lillian]]. The Greenheart [[thalos-briarwood]] permits her to let the party observe the troubled Yarrows at dusk (S2.12–13).

## Knowledge & motivations
Guarded but more forthcoming than the Greenheart, Anwen admits the Quickened are troubled because their natural place is "under pressure," that something has changed even if nothing changed on the town's side, and hints repeatedly that the cause lies far to the **west, deep beneath the lake** where larger Quickened dwell (S2.13). [[caspian-talon]] is keenly interested in her knowledge and her mention of divination.

## Appearances
- S2.12: Arranged to host the party's dusk observation of the Quickened.
- S2.13: Guides the observation; points west, beneath the lake, as the source.
