## Overview
Serris Dane is a young, fervent devotee at [[rhynharrans-hall]] in [[greymere]], first flagged when [[rell-aetris]]'s Detect Thoughts catches his abnormal excitement about the crisis (S2.10). He prays intensely at a private home shrine ([[serris-house]]) while his worried parents [[elric]] and [[berla]] look on, and has grown estranged from the temple, convinced he knows more than its priests do.

## Knowledge & motivations
Serris has suffered terrible recurring dreams of a monster for months — pre-dating the Quickened trouble (S2.14) — and is convinced [[rhynharran]] requires an offering or sacrifice. To answer this calling he secretly enlists the boatwright [[jorren]] to build a small boat warded only to survive storms (not to keep its occupants alive), intending to **recreate the [[the-stormblind]] voyage** and be swept up by his god (S2.13) — a plan that would almost certainly kill him. His father admits an ancestral gift: Elric's own father foretold bad weather through dreams.

The truth, revealed by the [[the-eldryn]] (S2.21): Serris's dreams are the **Eldryn's prophetic warnings reaching the wrong recipient**. The [[the-irdane|Irdane]] bloodline — the role of interpreting Eldryn messages for Greymere — diverged across a split succession, so both [[mathis-irdane]] and Serris (Serris Dane) carry pieces of it. Serris is not mad and not a mastermind, but a genuine, unwitting conduit. The party reconciles him and Mathis as joint Irdane (S2.24).

## Appearances
