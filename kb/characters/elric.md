## Overview
Elric is the father of [[serris-dane]] and husband of [[berla]] (S2.14). Worried about his son's months of prophetic monster-dreams, he admits that his own father had a reputation for foretelling bad weather through dreams — pointing to the ancestral gift that, the Eldryn later reveal, is the misdirected [[the-irdane|Irdane]] inheritance.

## Appearances
- S2.14: Reveals the family's dream-foretelling lineage to Mags and Rell.
