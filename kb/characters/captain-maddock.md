## Overview
Captain Maddock is the gnome commander of the [[griswald-garrison]], the mercenary company guarding the [[tower-of-heaven]]. Won over by the party, he smuggled them inside in mage's robes to confront [[pyarz]], and the party later proved [[shalfay]]'s survival to him and the garrison (S1.03).

## Appearances
- S1.03: Smuggles the party into the Tower; is shown proof the real High Mage lives.
