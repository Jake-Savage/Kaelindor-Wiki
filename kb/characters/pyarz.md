## Overview
Pyarz was the usurper who faked [[shalfay]]'s death and installed himself as acting High Mage of the [[tower-of-heaven]] (S1.03). He kept the petitioners' hall running without the Tower's leatherbound divination book, producing only useless riddles, and concealed that he bore no rank tattoos — which should change automatically with office.

## Knowledge & motivations
He was the inside hand of the plot against Shalfay, employer (via a recovered sending stone) of the shadow blade who hunted the fallen star. [[caspian-talon]] exposed his missing rank tattoos as proof Shalfay lived; in the ensuing battle Pyarz unleashed a spectator (beholder-kin) before [[trevick]] landed the killing blow.

## Appearances
- S1.03: Exposed by Caspian, fights the party, and is killed; his sending stone is recovered.

## DM notes / secrets
- His death should have ended the shadow blade's contract; the broader plot he served (and who, if anyone, was above him) is not pursued further in the sources.
