## Overview
Ethis is a centaur captain, the military second of [[hesk]] at the [[the-eldryn-camp]] (S2.18). He helps manage the camp, enforce the party's oath, and break up the Nameweave gathering when "uthrel" is spoken (S2.20).

## Appearances
- S2.18: Helps brief and bind the party with Hesk.
- S2.20–22: Manages the camp; involved in the patrol to the tampered gate.
