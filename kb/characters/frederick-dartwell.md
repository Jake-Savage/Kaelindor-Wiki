## Overview
Frederick Dartwell was a beloved bard of [[toadhop]], long dead and historical. His grave was robbed — minor bones taken, clawed scratch marks left — and his magical [[the-golden-lute]] stolen by quasits serving the devil [[joster-sirit]] (S1.05). The villagers' petition to recover the lute is what drew the second party together.

## Appearances
- S1.05: His robbed grave is the inciting mystery of the Toadhop arc.
