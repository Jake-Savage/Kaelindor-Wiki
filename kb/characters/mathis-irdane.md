## Overview
Mathis Irdane is the young (mid-twenties) new Lord of [[greymere]], ruling from [[irdane-hall]] with his wife [[skarris]]. He briefs the party on the [[the-quickened|Quickened]] crisis: the lake spirits called the Yarrows, normally harmless teasers of fishermen, have killed Bran Hosteigg and Orren Bell, the trouble apparently beginning around a "safety" charm made by the missing hedge-witch [[illyra-fen]] (S2.11). He refused [[hethan-kroll]]'s mercenary "solution" (S2.14).

## Knowledge & motivations
Mathis wants to protect his town and end the crisis, but feels boxed in: the [[the-warding-of-rhynharran|Greenheart]] [[thalos-briarwood]] counsels only patience, and he lacks the means to treat with the [[the-eldryn]]. The party's great gift to him is revealing why: the hereditary **[[the-irdane|Irdane]]** role — interpreting the Eldryn's messages for Greymere — diverged across a split succession, so Mathis holds only part of it and [[serris-dane]] (Serris Dane) carries the rest. Mathis is relieved to learn this and resolves to work with Serris as joint Irdane (S2.21, reconciled S2.24). He is quietly glad [[hethan-kroll]] is dead but disclaims ordering the killing.

## Appearances
- S2.10–11: Briefs the party on the Yarrows, the deaths, and [[illyra-fen]]'s charm.
- S2.14: Refuses Kroll's mercenaries.
- S2.21/24: Reconciled with [[serris-dane]] as joint Irdane; resolves to summon Serris and cooperate.
- S2.23–25: Vasha comes to him then vanishes; he is glad Kroll is dead; unhappy the mercenaries leave before the crisis fully resolves.

## DM notes / secrets
- Mathis disclaims ordering [[hethan-kroll]]'s Itharian-style ritual murder but is glad of it (S2.24); who actually killed Kroll is unresolved (the [[the-shadowed-path]]/[[itharis]] marks and the vanished [[vasha]] are clues).
