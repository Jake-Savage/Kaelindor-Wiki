## Overview
Mayor Duvesin was the last mayor of [[nerrin]], now dead and historical. The party found her journals in the mayor's house, descending from clinical reports into madness as the meenlock disappearances mounted (S1.01), alongside books on the Feywild and a map of the cave-in. The Duvesin family bears a snowdrop signet ring ([[duvesin-signet-ring]]), one of which was found on a meenlock corpse and later returned to descendants in Bridgemarch.

## Appearances
- S1.01: Her journals and signet ring are discovered in Nerrin, charting the town's fall.
