## Overview
The Gardener is a blue-skinned Archfey, robed in greenery, who guards the [[the-feywild-garden]] — the Feywild sanctuary reached through the frozen waterfall beyond the [[the-crystal-cave]] (S1.04). The garden was built 250 years ago by the Tower mage Perfora and her elven love Cerwin as a refuge; those who drink from its fountain may stay forever but can never return once they leave. The Gardener presides over its satyrs (led by [[zanim]]), unicorns ([[stargleam]], [[silverlily]]), and charmed residents.

## Knowledge & motivations
The Gardener is curious about [[dakir]] the construct but firmly protective of the sanctuary. When shown the moss, the unicorns are horrified and the Gardener **disintegrates the sample**, calling it a danger to the sanctuary and refusing all further discussion (S1.04) — an early, emphatic sign that the Feywild's own powers fear [[the-uthrel]]. [[caspian-talon]] later laments this destroyed his only specimen and speculates the Gardener would have had the skill to tamper with the [[the-bright-gate]]'s wards (S2.22).

## DM notes / secrets
- The Gardener's skill is invoked by [[caspian-talon]] (S2.22) as the kind of fey power capable of the gate-tampering — a possible (unconfirmed) lead in the `who-tampered-the-gate` thread, though the actual tamperer is tied to the [[the-furnace-brood]] / Korrathians.
