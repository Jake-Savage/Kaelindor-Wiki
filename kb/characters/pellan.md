## Overview
Pellan is the heraldmaster of the College of Arms who began processing [[benny-carter]]'s claim to be the legitimised heir of [[edward-harrington]] (S2.04). Because the claim was irregular, it triggered a petition to the Queen — which ultimately succeeded.
