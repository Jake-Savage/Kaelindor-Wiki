## Overview
Shalfay is the rightful High Mage of the [[tower-of-heaven]] and head of the [[order-of-the-tower-of-heaven]]. The party first encounters him only through harvested memories (S1.02) — a vision of a star falling "scarred by the heavens" and orders to seek it and to send a Bestiary to the druid [[derwyth]]. Presumed dead during [[pyarz]]'s coup, he is found alive in the observatory, haggard and clutching his great leatherbound divination book, and is restored to power by the party (S1.03).

## Knowledge & motivations
Shalfay is a scholar of prophecy and divination who fears "evil seizing the power of prophecy." He sought [[the-fallen-star]] as "the key to the tower's future," then sent the party to trade it to the deep gnomes for their [[books-of-prophecy]] (S1.03). He uses those books to crudely answer [[caspian-talon]]'s questions, returning an emphatic warning against the moss/contamination (S1.04). He remains [[caspian-talon]]'s standing divination contact — and a future destination, since the books the party donated to the Tower are reported to be somehow "wrong"/off (S2.23), a puzzle drawing the party back.

## Appearances
- S1.02: His harvested memories set the fallen-star quest in motion.
- S1.03: Found alive and restored as High Mage after [[pyarz]] is exposed and killed.
- S1.04: Rewards the party; divines a warning about the moss/[[the-uthrel]].
- S2.23: Via Sending, reports that the donated [[books-of-prophecy]] are "wrong" and that the uthrel name was helpful.

## DM notes / secrets
- The "wrong books" Shalfay reports (S2.23) are an open thread pointing toward a future return to the [[tower-of-heaven]] and possibly toward how prophecy/divination has been corrupted.
