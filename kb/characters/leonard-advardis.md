## Overview
Leonard Advardis is a sorcerer of the [[the-advardis-family]] bloodline who, despite his magical lineage, lacks magic of his own. The party met him among the petitioners at the [[tower-of-heaven]], where he helped reveal that the Tower's divinations had been failing under [[pyarz]] (S1.03).

## Appearances
- S1.03: Encountered as a petitioner; contributes to the party's understanding of the failing divinations.
