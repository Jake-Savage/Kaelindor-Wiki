## Overview
Joster Sirit is a devil who posed as a tiefling and, three months before the party's arrival, installed himself at [[paliset-hall]], dismissing the guards and tightening security (S1.05). Using quasits, he robbed the grave of the bard [[frederick-dartwell]] to steal the [[the-golden-lute]], and he enthralled [[lord-adilaire]] to sign away his lands (to a "Nymara Foss"), feeding on his victims through enchanted portraits. He is a collector of magical artifacts and keeps an extradimensional hoard.

## Knowledge & motivations
Joster collects magical objects and power, draining enthralled nobles. His deeper significance to the party is personal: during the Paliset Hall fight he recognised [[trevick]] as "a Salmeros child" and revealed he **knew Trevick's father** (S1.05). He resurfaces in S2.14 with a note referencing Trevick's father and enclosing his late mother's silver [[joster-pendant]] — knowledge of, and leverage over, Trevick's hidden heritage being his evident play. He was driven back to the hells (by [[dakir]]'s [[wand-of-lightning-bolts]]) but **not destroyed**.
