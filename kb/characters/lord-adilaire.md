## Overview
Lord Adilaire is the manor lord of [[paliset-hall]], who grew reclusive after his wife's death and held secretive parties (S1.05). The devil [[joster-sirit]] arrived three months before the party, dismissed the local guards, and placed Adilaire under magical thrall — using enchanted portraits to drain him and making him sluggishly sign his lands away to a "Nymara Foss." The party freed him by driving Joster back to the hells.

## Appearances
- S1.05: Found enthralled at Paliset Hall; freed when [[joster-sirit]] is banished; thanks the party.
