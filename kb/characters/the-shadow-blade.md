## Overview
The Shadow Blade is a shadow-magic mercenary, accompanied by a dog and bird of shadow, who interrogated [[caspian-talon]] and [[dakir]] about the harvested memories — both lied successfully and he departed (S1.02). He hunted [[the-fallen-star]] as a tool of the faction plotting against [[shalfay]] from inside the [[tower-of-heaven]].

## Knowledge & motivations
He worked for [[pyarz]]'s faction (the recovered sending stone links to his employer). With Pyarz dead, his contract should have ended (S1.03).

## Appearances
- S1.02: Interrogates Caspian and Dakir, then leaves.
- S1.03: His employer ([[pyarz]]) is killed; his contract presumed ended.
