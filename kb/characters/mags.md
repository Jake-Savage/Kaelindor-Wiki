## Overview
Mags — full name Smagslys Mørktbarn — is a gnome Cleric of [[naelos]] who joins the party at the Toadhop job (S1.05), introduced as the "aunt" of [[orin-njord]]. Centuries old and raised in the lightless Underdark, she is the party's blunt "parent": its primary healer, diplomatic anchor, and moral compass. She greets the lake spirits with offerings, befriends [[nessa]], and becomes the party's bridge to the [[the-eldryn]]. She is the faith's exemplar of [[the-covenant-of-naelos]] — community, oaths, and transparent dealing.

## Knowledge & motivations
Mags sees the Greymere crisis as exactly the eye-of-the-needle Naelos expects her to thread: "the one tiny route that doesn't lead to one side or the other or both slaughtering each other" (S2.13). Her Underdark upbringing — a tightly controlled world that left offerings for nameless things and politely exiled anyone who upset the balance — makes her uniquely able to read the [[the-eldryn]] and to grasp how recklessly "surface dwellers" provoke the [[the-prior|Prior]]. She is openly contemptuous of how Greymere "sent teenagers off into the woods as emissaries" and ignored the spirits until forced to negotiate.

She deliberately times the party's diplomacy around **Nameweave** (a holy day of "honest and transparent dealing"), uneasily aware she is half-using a high holiday to "trick people into helping you" — and falls back on getting everyone drunk when she can't recall the rites (S2.18). Tellingly, her chapter-16 diary is written entirely in the voice of the wood she was petrified into — a stream of sap and roots and light — capturing her time as a tree. She also carries deep Underdark lore on the uthrel: a childhood nursery rhyme ("For Uthrel walks the underways / And waits for you beneath") and the gnomish "Ordinances for We Þat Dwell Beneþe Þe Eorþe," which forbid touching strange mosses and breaking unknown seals.

## DM notes / secrets
- Her undead-tinged bone shard (a Naelos relic of her own bones) keys the infernal "half in this world and half of another" doors in the Shifting Library (S2.08) — a detail worth remembering for her nature/longevity.
- She is the party member most reliably alert to deception (Zone of Truth, Calm Emotions, Augury) and is the one who first senses both [[caspian-talon]]'s and [[dakir]]'s concealments.
