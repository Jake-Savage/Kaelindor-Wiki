## Overview
Garron Corwen is a spy who, with his shift partner [[osric-crow]], watched the party on [[lord-torston]]'s orders. Subdued after a chaotic magical scuffle (S2.03), he confessed and — more frightened of Torston than of the party — agreed to feed Osric a bland account and tip the party off, then staged an escape as the city watch arrived.
