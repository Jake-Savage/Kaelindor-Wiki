## Overview
Tamzin Venik is named in one of three Furnace Brood instruction-scrolls the party recovered (S2.27). Per the scroll, she is a travelling merchant told to head north to Rivers Meet and on toward the mountains — **openly, not in secret** — carrying five magical tokens that act as beacons to those who know them. She is to meet "the hunters," verify they have placed caches, and leave one token in each cache. The party has not met her; all of this is from the scroll.
