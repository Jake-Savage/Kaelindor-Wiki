## Overview
Jorren is a boatwright of [[greymere]] who works from [[the-boatshed]], secretly helping [[serris-dane]] strengthen a small rowboat with "storm marks" (S2.13). The boat is warded only to stay afloat in storms, with no provision for keeping its occupants alive — built so Serris can recreate the [[the-stormblind]] expedition.

## Appearances
- S2.13: Found building Serris's storm-worthy boat.
