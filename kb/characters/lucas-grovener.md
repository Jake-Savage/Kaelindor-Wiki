## Overview
Lucas Grovener is the innkeeper of the Bored Weasel in [[stonehaven]] and a survivor of the [[nerrin]] mining disaster; his wife Lorna died in the cave-in. He asked the party to lay flowers on Lorna's grave and retrieve a locket she hid in Nerrin's offering tree (S1.01), and in gratitude gifted [[trevick]] the magical [[trevicks-crossbow]] (S1.02).

## Appearances
- S1.01: Recounts Nerrin's creeping terror; asks the grave-favour; draws the party a map.
- S1.02: Gifts [[trevick]] a magical crossbow after the favour is completed.
