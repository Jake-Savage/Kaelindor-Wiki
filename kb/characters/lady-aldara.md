## Overview
Lady Aldara is the ruler of [[stonehaven]] and the party's first patron, hiring them to assess and clear the abandoned mining town of [[nerrin]] so she could reopen its platinum mine and avoid raising war taxes (S1.01). She is pragmatic and academically curious, intrigued by [[dakir]] the construct, and presides with her son and heir [[edric-vayne]].

## Appearances
- S1.01: Hires the party for the Nerrin job; recounts the town's history.
- S1.02: Asks the party to return [[duvesin-signet-ring]] to descendants in Bridgemarch.
- S1.04: Settled with after Nerrin is confirmed cleansed and the mine reopened.
