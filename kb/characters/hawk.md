## Overview
Hawk is the spirit hawk of [[trevick]], used throughout the campaign as a scout (retrieving Lorna's locket at Nerrin in S1.01; tracking the Eldryn patrols in S2.19–20). To the firbolgs of the [[the-eldryn]], however, Hawk is no ordinary bird: they react with reverence, naming him a **[[the-watcher|Watcher]]** — a concentrated spirit that [[rhynharran]] sends to watch over those who must witness or do something important, and which has watched Trevick's family across generations (S2.19).

## Knowledge & motivations
As a spirit companion, Hawk acts on Trevick's bond, but the firbolgs frame him as an agent of [[rhynharran]] marking a chosen lineage — "both an honour and a burden." Trevick begins to sense Hawk is more than a beast.

## Appearances
- S1.01: Retrieves Lorna's locket from Nerrin's offering tree.
- S2.19: Revealed by the firbolgs as a Rhynharran Watcher tied to Trevick's family.
- S2.20: Scouts the warded burnt zone (the [[the-hallow-burn]]) with [[theodore]].

## DM notes / secrets
- Hawk is a true [[the-watcher|Watcher]] of [[rhynharran]], central to [[trevick]]'s destiny and the open `trevicks-watcher` thread; the Eldryn weigh its significance (S2.21).
