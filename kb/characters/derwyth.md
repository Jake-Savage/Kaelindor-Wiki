## Overview
Derwyth is a self-exiled elf druid and "star-watcher" living in [[cernant-valley]], guarded by her giant eagles [[avri]] and [[ivo]] and her sabretooth tiger [[selice]] (S1.02). She fled the elvish empire and refuses to discuss why.

## Knowledge & motivations
She identified the harvested memories' subject as High Mage [[shalfay]], warned of the plot to oust him, and — accepting a Bestiary as payment — directed the party to the derro raiding camp at the fallen star's crash site to recover [[the-fallen-star]]. She named the meddling "hand of [[rhynharran]]" felt in the region's eighteen months of unrest.

## Appearances
- S1.02: Tests then welcomes the party; locates the star and sends them to the derro camp; befriends [[trevick]] to [[selice]].
