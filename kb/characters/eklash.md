## Overview
Eklash is a deep-gnome warden encountered around the [[deep-gnome-forge]]. He recognised the black moss sample but treated it as taboo, revealing that the deep gnomes have "a similar problem" with it in the under-dark — an early hint of [[the-uthrel]]'s reach (S1.02–03). The gnomes believed, wrongly, that it could not spread on the surface.

## Appearances
- S1.02: First recognises the moss but treats it as taboo.
- S1.03: Confirms the under-dark moss problem during the star trade.
