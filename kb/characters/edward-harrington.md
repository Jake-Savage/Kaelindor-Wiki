## Overview
Lord Edward Harrington was a noble found drained of blood in the [[silver-moon-bathhouse]], his death blamed on a vampire (S2.01). The investigation reveals the killing was **staged** — body displayed, neck wound exposed, blood flushed down the drain — to disguise a murder as a vampire feeding. He was ambitious, loyal, and the rival of [[lord-torston]].

## Knowledge & motivations
Harrington was secretly building a **treason case against [[lord-torston]]**, intending to bring his evidence to Princess [[princess-orlaith|Órlaith]], Commander of the Bridgeguard, to gain the upper hand (S2.02). He corresponded with the vampire [[lucien]] as part of this conspiracy, alongside [[damar]] and [[bartholomew-crane]]. By a genuine signed deed he named the unrelated [[benny-carter]] his natural son and heir. His corpse was questioned via Speak with Dead before the party could reach it (S2.03).

## Appearances
- S2.01: His staged murder opens the investigation.
- S2.02: His scheme, his protégé Benny, and his hidden study evidence emerge.
- S2.03: His letters expose Torston; his corpse is found already questioned.

## DM notes / secrets
- Harrington was murdered by [[lord-torston]] to bury his treason case; the vampire frame was Torston's cover. See [[lord-torston]].
