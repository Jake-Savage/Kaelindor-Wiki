## Overview
Sidney Pollard is a dragonborn fighter and farmer who escorted the villagers of [[toadhop]] to [[queen-rowenna]]'s Hearing Day to petition for help recovering [[frederick-dartwell]]'s stolen [[the-golden-lute]] (S1.05). He joined the newly-formed party for that investigation but has been largely inactive since the early arc. Fabian fondly remembers him as "a maestro on the spoons" (S2.20).

## Knowledge & motivations
Sidney's motivation is straightforward and local: he brought the Toadhop villagers' grievance to the Queen and saw it through. He has no known stake in the wider Uthrel or Korrathian plots.
