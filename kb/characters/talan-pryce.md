## Overview
Talan Pryce is an artificer-priest of the [[church-of-calvyr]] who completed [[dakir]]'s [[dakirs-spell-storing-device]] using the components [[bartholomew-crane]] left behind — the device now stores low- to mid-level spells (S2.10).

## Appearances
- S2.10: Finishes Dakir's spell-storing device at the Church of Calvyr.

## DM notes / secrets
- As a Calvyr artificer-priest working on [[dakir]]'s internal device, Pryce is connected to the church that built Dakir the construct; a potential source of knowledge about Dakir's true nature.
