## Overview
Captain Thoronby is a Bridgemarch guard captain whose squad — equipped against elemental magic on [[oscar-wicklow]]'s order — raided the [[the-tiefling-shop]], the warded Korrathian contact point (S2.26). A ward-blast injured his first soldiers and the two tiefling cultists killed themselves with acid before capture; the party then uncovered a blood-and-ash shrine to the [[the-furnace-brood]] behind the fireplace.
