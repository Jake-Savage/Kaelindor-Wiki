## Overview
Theodore is the rat familiar of [[caspian-talon]] — a frequently-used scout and spy who can be sent invisibly to overhear plans and gather intelligence. Caspian relies on him repeatedly (notably eavesdropping on the [[the-eldryn]] camp's warding plans, S2.19) and is wryly fond of him despite his "I hate to ask Theo to offer his services" grumbling.

## Knowledge & motivations
As a familiar, Theodore acts on Caspian's direction. Nameweave is noted (in Caspian's diary) as "Theo's favourite holiday."

## Appearances
- S1.01–05: Caspian's familiar through the early arc.
- S2.03: Killed by [[bartholomew-crane]] when investigating the Brass Tower clocktower; later resummoned.
- S2.04: Returns terrified bearing Caspian's letter after Caspian's secret meeting with [[lucien]].
- S2.19–20: Scouts the Eldryn camp and the burnt zone invisibly alongside [[hawk]].
