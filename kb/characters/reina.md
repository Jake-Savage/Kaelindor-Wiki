## Overview
Reina is a weathered druid (one of [[the-weathered]]) and the third of the Greenheart [[thalos-briarwood]]'s messengers sent to the [[the-eldryn]] — none of whom returned. Her [[reinas-ring]] (a Ring of Protection +1 with druidic abjuration) reached [[trevick]] through the forest, identifying her as its true owner and prompting the Greenheart to commission Trevick's mission (S2.15). She is found safe in the [[the-eldryn-camp]], where she and [[elset]] are housed with the party (S2.18).

## Knowledge & motivations
Reina explains the Eldryn distrust outsiders entrusted with forest secrets and that the forest is ancient and once far larger (S2.19). She hints the Eldryn regard the situation as a great tragedy and danger but won't say more. The party returns her safely toward Greymere (S2.23).
