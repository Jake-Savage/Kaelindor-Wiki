## Overview
Gladys Henshaw owns the [[silver-moon-bathhouse]] where [[edward-harrington]] was found dead (S2.01). She let the party in to investigate and revealed that the body had been **deliberately displayed**, neck wound exposed, and that Harrington had been boastful about a scheme he was close to pulling off — early clues that the murder was staged.

## Appearances
- S2.01: Admits the party; reveals the staged display and Harrington's boasting.
