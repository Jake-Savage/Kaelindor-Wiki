## Overview
Nessa is a firbolg-blooded hedge-witch and potion-maker living on the western edge of [[greymere]] ([[nessas-cottage]]), able to guide travellers into the [[the-eldryn-forest]] (S2.11). She befriends [[mags]] and [[trevick]], teaching Trevick to read the forest's subtle trail markers and becoming the party's trusted conduit toward the [[the-eldryn]].

## Knowledge & motivations
Nessa knows the forest intimately and is troubled by what is happening to it: the trail markers have been changing faster and more unpredictably for about two seasons, many more areas are now forbidden, and in spring she smelled faint smoke from a distant forest fire to the **west** — coinciding with the markers growing unstable (S2.12). Many markers are now burnt or blackened (S2.13). She speaks warmly of the missing [[illyra-fen]], noting Illyra's elven blood and the prejudice driving her away, and trusts she is safe. Nessa's guidance and Trevick's offerings together draw the forest's response — the [[the-carved-wren]], [[reinas-ring]], and [[the-owl-feather]] (S2.14).

When Greymere grows tense and outsiders are scapegoated, Nessa abandons her cottage and shelters at [[rhynharrans-hall]] among the townsfolk to avoid being targeted (S2.23).
