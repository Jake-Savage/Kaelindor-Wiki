## Overview
Annet Brigg succeeds [[hethan-kroll]] as leader of the [[krolls-mercenaries]] after his murder — though the band remains magically loyal to the dead Kroll (S2.25). [[fabian-perennius]] negotiates with her, persuading the band to return the 406 gold they extorted from [[greymere]] in exchange for a writ to his brother Quintus Palaveus in [[crownspire]] and 200 gold in travel expenses; the mercenaries then march south.

## Appearances
- S2.25: Accepts Fabian's writ-and-expenses deal; leads the band away to Crownspire.
