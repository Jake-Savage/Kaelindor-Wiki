## Overview
Corbin Dunn is a footman dismissed from [[paliset-hall]] who became the party's informant (S1.05). He revealed that [[lord-adilaire]] had grown reclusive since his wife's death and held secretive parties, and that a tiefling named [[joster-sirit]] arrived three months earlier, dismissed the local guards, and tightened security.

## Appearances
- S1.05: Informs the party about Adilaire and Joster at the Toadhop inn.
