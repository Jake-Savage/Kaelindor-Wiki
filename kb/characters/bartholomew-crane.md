## Overview
Bartholomew Crane is a gnome ex-paladin of [[church-of-calvyr]] who ran the [[the-brass-tower]] clocktower and protected the vampire [[lucien]] (S2.03). A co-conspirator with [[edward-harrington]] and [[damar]] against [[lord-torston]], he was acting under Lucien's charm and refused to abandon the vampire.

## Knowledge & motivations
Frightened, he killed [[theodore]] when the familiar investigated the clocktower, then agreed to talk: he confirmed Torston was a traitor selling Kaelindor's secrets and that the conspirators gathered evidence, but refused to explain "0950" and asked for two days (S2.03). He helped [[caspian-talon]] free Lucien and flee the city, and left behind the artificer components that [[talan-pryce]] later used to finish [[dakir]]'s [[dakirs-spell-storing-device]].

## Appearances
- S2.03: Kills Theodore; confesses Torston's treason and his loyalty to Lucien.
- S2.05: Helps free [[lucien]]; leaves Dakir's components; flees the city.
