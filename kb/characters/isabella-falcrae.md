## Overview
Isabella Falcrae is a student of the [[order-of-the-tower-of-heaven]] who, with her lover [[bran-oris]], absconded into [[the-crystal-cave]] and its [[the-feywild-garden]] three months before the party arrived, seeking to escape arranged duties (S1.04). Found charmed and happy, she was persuaded (by Bran) to leave. Back at the Tower she coldly burned her farewell letter — a sign the fey contentment had been broken on departure.

## Appearances
- S1.04: Found in the Feywild garden; escorted home; burns her letter on return.
