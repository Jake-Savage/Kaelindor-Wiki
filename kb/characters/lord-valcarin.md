## Overview
Lord Valcarin is the current ruler of [[trevaryn|Trevaryn]], seated at [[drake-mount|Drake Mount]].
Trevaryn is governed by **eight leading families** who make up its ruling council and elect a
ruler for a **twenty-five-year term** (a longer term than [[greywatch|Greywatch]]'s twelve). Because
the Mirrowen is effectively a client of Trevaryn, no one governs Greywatch without the approval of
Valcarin and the Drake Mount council; he is **close to the outgoing Warden [[ivo-pierron|Ivo
Pierron]]** and will have a hand in choosing his successor.

## Appearances
- S2.29: Named in town gossip in [[greywatch]] as Trevaryn's ruler whose approval shapes the
  Warden succession. Not met in person.
