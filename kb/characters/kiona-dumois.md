## Overview
Kiona Dumois was an undead tiefling necromancer who created and ruled the [[the-shifting-library]] — a planar subspace that travels between the Material Plane and the hells, nesting in real libraries so her spectral figures can steal books (S2.07). Her soul was shattered in an accident involving the [[codex-mortis]], a tome on reanimating the dead, which transformed her into a powerful spectral undead.

## Knowledge & motivations
Kiona's single obsession was recovering the lost [[codex-mortis]]. Her library was a vast harvesting engine — staffed by spectral scholars, defended by rune-bound skeletons, oozes, and exploding cold-light lamps, and built around a reconstructable, sacrifice-fuelled portal to the hells (S2.08). She greeted the party from her desk asking what they had "brought her," still treating intruders as suppliers of books.

## Appearances
- S2.07: The party is pulled into her Shifting Library; her nature and purpose are uncovered.
- S2.08: Her sacrificial chamber and hell portal are found and sabotaged; her spectral apparition is confronted.
- S2.09: Destroyed in a long attrition battle ([[trevick]]'s arrows land the kill); she leaves no corpse and her library collapses.

## DM notes / secrets
- Her infernal notes on the [[codex-mortis]] passed to [[caspian-talon]] — dangerous necromantic knowledge that yields secrets over time and keeps the Codex Mortis in play even though the book itself is lost.
