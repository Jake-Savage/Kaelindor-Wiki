## Overview
Orin Njord is a goliath Paladin and the nephew of [[mags]], joining the party for the Toadhop job (S1.05). He is nervous and devout, and notably speaks gnomish (his aunt's tongue). Like [[sidney-pollard]], he became minor after the early arc and does not feature in the Greymere/Uthrel storyline.

## Knowledge & motivations
Orin is driven by faith and family loyalty, tied to [[the-covenant-of-naelos]] through [[mags]]. His specific agenda beyond piety is not detailed in the sources.

## Appearances
- S1.05: Joins the party (with his aunt [[mags]]) for the Toadhop investigation and the fight against [[joster-sirit]].
