## Overview
Queen Rowenna is the monarch of [[kaelindor]] and Warden of the Silent Road (the realm whose elite are the [[knights-of-the-silent-road]]). She holds a monthly **Hearing Day** at which subjects bring petitions — the occasion at which the [[toadhop]] villagers' plea for the stolen [[the-golden-lute]] is heard and the second party is formed (S1.05).

## Knowledge & motivations
As sovereign she dispenses justice and commissions through her court (her Chancellor [[oscar-wicklow]] acts as broker, and petitions such as [[benny-carter]]'s legitimisation rise to her). Her direct agenda is not detailed in the sources, but her institutions sit at the centre of several plots: [[lord-torston]] falsified the records of her [[knights-of-the-silent-road]], and her circle is the object of Torston's unexplained fixation.

## DM notes / secrets
- The crown's institutions are targets of the treason and conspiracy plots: Torston's falsified Silent Road records and war-tithe accounting (S2.05) and his interest in "the Queen's circle" (S2.06) all point back toward her court.
