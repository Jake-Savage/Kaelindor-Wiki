## Overview
Meera was the wife of [[jorrith-potter|Jorrith Potter]]. The two left [[greywatch]] for about a
decade, then returned together and set up the food business that became the
[[the-three-copper-hatch|Three Copper Hatch]]. By the gossip the party gathers (S2.29), she was the
cheerful, sociable driving force behind it — well-liked, good at getting the trade established —
where Jorrith was always the quieter, wearier of the two. They had no children. She **died of a
quick, unidentified illness** after about three years back in town; Jorrith has carried on alone
and joylessly since. As the Mirrowen does not bury its dead (bodies are cremated), her remains were
burned. A framed drawing of her, made when she was young, sits by Jorrith's bed.

Townsfolk **disagree on whether she was a tiefling** — she had "a touch of soot about her" but no
obvious horns or tail — which the DM frames as consistent with **distant (later-generation)
tiefling descent**, where planar traits fade after a few generations away from [[korrathian|Karath]].
