## Overview
Elset is a weathered druid (one of [[the-weathered]]) found safe in the [[the-eldryn-camp]] alongside [[reina]], housed with the party (S2.18). Like Reina, Elset hints the Eldryn regard the crisis as a great tragedy and danger but says little more, content to wait it out. The party escorts both back toward Greymere (S2.23).

## Appearances
- S2.18: Found alive in the Eldryn camp.
