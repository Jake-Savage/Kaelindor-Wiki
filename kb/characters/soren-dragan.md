## Overview
Soren Dragan is named in one of three Furnace Brood instruction-scrolls the party recovered from the Korrathian [[the-tiefling-shop|shop]] (S2.27). Per the scroll, he is an adventurer or treasure-hunter instructed to travel overland through northern [[offendale|Offendale]] to the Tangles (Meroen) and meet a contact in [[greywatch|Greywatch]] to pursue a rumour — quickly, in secret, and expecting danger. An address was enclosed. The party has not yet met him; everything known is from the scroll.
