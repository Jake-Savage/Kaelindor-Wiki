## Overview
Bran Oris is a student of the [[order-of-the-tower-of-heaven]] who absconded into [[the-feywild-garden]] with his lover [[isabella-falcrae]] (S1.04). Pressed by the party to question whether they were happy or merely prisoners, Bran convinced Isabella to leave; unlike her, he kept his farewell letter on returning home. He gave the party his signet ring for his family.

## Appearances
- S1.04: Found in the Feywild garden; convinces Isabella to leave; escorted home.
