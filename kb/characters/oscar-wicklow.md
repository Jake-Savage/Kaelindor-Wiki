## Overview
Oscar Wicklow is the Chancellor of Kaelindor, based in [[bridgemarch]], who personally briefs the party on the [[greymere]] fishing crisis and frames the mission as a test of them (S2.10). He explains "the Prior" (the [[the-quickened|Quickened]] and the [[the-eldryn]]) and offers official favour plus 1400 gold. He is rumoured to be a **secret priest of [[itharis]]** ([[the-shadowed-path]]).

## Knowledge & motivations
Wicklow presents as a pragmatic, testing patron, but he conceals real knowledge. When the party returns and reports, he treats the Korrathian cultists in the city seriously, has Captain [[captain-thoronby]]'s squad raid the cell, surmises the suicided tieflings were a contact point rather than attackers, and renegotiates the party's pay (1400g for Greymere, 400g for the new task) — and takes **keen interest in [[krolls-map]]**, keeping it (S2.26). Critically, [[mags]] and [[caspian-talon]] catch him **lying when he denies knowing of the uthrel** — he plainly does know.

## Appearances

## DM notes / secrets
- **Wicklow lies about not knowing the uthrel** (S2.26), and his interest in [[krolls-map]] makes his concealed knowledge a major open suspicion — possibly tying the Chancellor's office (and the rumoured [[itharis|Itharian]] connection) to the [[the-korrathian-conspiracy]] or to whoever benefits from suppressing knowledge of the Uthrel. The chapter's central unresolved figure.
