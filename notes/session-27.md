# Session 27

*Date unrecorded. Reconstructed from a player's spoken recap — session 27 was not fully recorded — so this account is thinner and less certain than transcript-derived notes.*

Dakir informs the party that he is leaving. He states he is struggling with his identity and has questions to answer, including learning more about dragons after the remains the party encountered in the Eldryn forest. He wishes them well, says he hopes to meet again, and departs.

## The Tiefling Shop

The remaining party returns to the raided Korrathian shop. The city guards are still searching it, and the magic dampener is still active.

The party goes first to the trapped chests, as Caspian has since learned Dispel Magic. His first attempt fails and injures him; a second, with Mags and Fabian assisting, succeeds. Fabian fails to lift one box, so Mags has the guards carry it outside, where she opens it. The contents are further trapped.

Rell investigates the contents, focusing on a set of magically protected documents. Augmented by Mags, he determines the protection can be circumvented but not how to do so at once. Caspian studies the magic and shares his findings; he and Rell confirm that Rell's amulet will serve — it does not disable the traps, but makes the ward less able to register Rell as the wrong recipient. With it, Rell opens all the documents. The party tells the guards they are taking the papers for study and will share the results; Caspian writes a receipt and promises a translation.

## Beneath the kitchen

Fabian, guided by Mags, traces the dampening effect to the kitchen and the flagstones beneath it, and has the guards and Rell lever up the stone. Below is a blackened stone carved with magical runes. Moving it makes the rituals worked here evident, and the magical aura becomes nauseating.

Fabian and Rell find scratches and other physical signs. Rell casts Detect Thoughts for mental or emotional imprints and finds psychic traces of emotion — a general sense of suffering — without resolvable detail. The residual magic and the suffering cause Rell and Caspian physical pain; Rell withstands it better. Caspian identifies the magics used: communication; the renewal of bonds, oaths, or pledges of service and allegiance; and possibly exchange, tribute, or offerings. He reads some of the suffering as inflicted for research alone.

Rell projects the residual emotions and memories into the room for the others. Fabian and Mags perceive them; Caspian declines further exposure. Rell presses for clearer memories, the pain intensifies, and after Mags heals him and a third attempt fails, he stops before it can kill him.

## Snowdrop House

The party translates the papers — three Furnace Brood instruction-scrolls:

- **To Hethan Kroll:** travel south by water through Offendale to the town of Fairfield, and to the manor of the noble Amir Merillac, who has been working for the Furnace Brood but whom the Brood now suspects. Kroll is to bring his best people as security and watch for the noble double-crossing the Brood.
- **To Tamzin Venik** (a travelling merchant): head north to Rivers Meet and on to the mountains, openly and not in secret, carrying five magical tokens that act as beacons to those who know them; meet "the hunters," verify they have placed caches, and leave one token in each.
- **To Soren Dragan** (an adventurer or treasure-hunter): travel overland through northern Offendale to the Tangles (Meroen) and meet a contact in Greywatch to pursue a rumour — quickly, in secret, and expecting danger. An address is enclosed.

The party resolves to pursue Soren Dragan to Greywatch. They check in with Torston, asking whether his reported new position is genuine and whether he can access the evidence of a conspiracy. Torston says the investigation should continue and does not comment on the position or the evidence.
